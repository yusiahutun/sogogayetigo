Zendaya's Archival &quot;Challengers&quot; Press Look Included a Surprise Fluffy Tail

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Leave it to Zendaya to keep us on our toes. For her latest appearance in support of her upcoming movie Challengers, the actor and stylist Law Roach pulled an ensemble from the Vivienne Westwood Spring/Summer ’94 ready-to-wear collection, which they gave a few small adjustments to give it a more sporty feel (while thankfully preserving one eye-catching detail).

Originally, the blue-and-red pinstripe skirt suit felt more office-ready, as it was composed of a long-sleeved blazer and matching miniskirt. Law had the long sleeves and large lapels removed from the top to give it a more flexible silhouette, and bring it closer to the look of a tennis dress.

Jeff Spicer//Getty Images

As is often the case with Westwood designs, a surprise was in store with this particular set, which could be categorized as business in the front, party in the back. While posing for photographers at the London photo call, Zendaya made sure to turn around and show off the feathery white accessory attached to the back of the skirt, which looked just like a bunny tail. (Remember when Carrie Bradshaw wore an equally thrilling white scut on Sex and the City?) It’s hard to pull off a fluffy tail, but if anyone can do it, it’s Zendaya.

Gareth Cattermole//Getty Images

To match the fluffy accoutrement, the Dune actor slipped into a pair of pearly white stiletto heels, and added another flash of white with her French tip nails. For accessories, the Emmy winner kept it simple with subtle golden jewelry from Bulgari. She had her blonde hair tied up in a bouncy ponytail (which evoked even more hopping-related imagery, if the tail didn’t quite do it). Who knows what the next Challengers press appearance will bring?
