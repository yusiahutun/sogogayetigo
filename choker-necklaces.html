As much as I dread putting away my summer clothes and accessories, only to prepare for the upcoming cold, there’s one thing I always get just a teensy bit excited for and that’s finding cute jewelry to layer for fall. There’s nothing like picking out two or three pieces and placing them atop a gorgeous sweater or cardigan. The choices are pretty much endless and regardless of your style, it can be so fun to figure out what combinations work best to suit your personal flair.

Our suggestion if you’re trying to layer jewelry? Go for a choker. This type of necklace wears a bit higher on your neckline, adding dimension and ensuring a tangle-free mess when wearing more than one piece.

If you want to know the best place to get high-quality yet affordable (and still stunning) choker necklaces, we’ll let you in on our little secret—you’ve got to start shopping at Hello Adorn. The jewelry brand delivers incredible, handmade jewelry at prices that won’t break the bank (we’re talking choker necklaces starting at $52).

We’ve done some digging on Hello Adorn’s site and found eight chokers we’re absolutely obsessed with—they’re modern, bold, and affordable. Keep scrolling to shop our top choker necklace picks to stun in this fall.

  • Lace Choker

    This dainty choker necklace is the epitome of elegance and charm. The Lace Choker’s light-catching gold 14-karat chain reflects stunning shine and is handmade to boot. Its simplicity makes it the perfect layering piece to wear with any longer necklace, like the beautiful green gemstone one shown on the model.

  • Ginny Choker

    If you’re looking for something more on the minimalist side, go for this sweet and darling piece. Made with 14-karat gold (or sterling silver if you’re more into cooler-toned jewelry), this choker is a timeless necklace that will last for the long haul.

  • Pearl Choker

    The elegant Pearl Choker can be mixed and matched, and we think it adapts to anyone’s ever-changing style. Pair it with a crisp white shirt for a polished look or let it peek out from a plunging neckline for a touch of allure.

  • Stevie Choker

    The Stevie Choker embodies the perfect blend between delicate and bold—it’s beautifully placed somewhere in the middle. Not to mention, it’ll blend seamlessly into any autumn OOTD.

  • Dylan Choker

    For something a little more in the higher price range, may we suggest the Dylan Choker? This chain is a bit thicker than the others: exquisite, luxe, and the perfect gift to give a friend or loved one to show your care for them.

  • Confetti Choker

    Transform your everyday necklace into a more fun and whimsical statement piece with this add-on. The Confetti Choker looks a bit different from some of the others we’ve listed, but we still think its rounded chain adds something unique and we love it.

  • Mari Choker

    Have you ever seen something so delicate and dainty? The Mari Choker’s textured bars are linked together with a very fine chain, adding sparkle and shine to your neckline. This radiant companion piece will pair perfectly with other minimalist pieces or any bold jewelry you’ve got in your collection.

  • Josie Choker

    Hello Adorn’s jewelry is built to last and crafted with care, so you can rest assured you’ll receive an amazing product you’ll cherish forever. Our favorite part about the Josie Choker? It’s adjustable, so you can wear it longer if you’d like.
