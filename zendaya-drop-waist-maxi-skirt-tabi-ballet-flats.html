Zendaya Gets in the Mood for Spring in a Midi Skirt and Ballet Flats

October 2024 · 2 minute read

Essays could be—and have been—written about Zendaya’s impeccable red-carpet style. The movie star is known for topping best-dressed lists every time she graces a flashy event, be it a film premiere or an award show. And while her street style is comparatively more relaxed and low-key than, say, a Mugler suit of armor or a vintage Versace couture gown, it’s still just as worthy of high praise.

Maison Margiela Tabi Leather Ballerina Shoes

Maison Margiela Tabi Leather Ballerina Shoes

To prove that point, she headed out for a casual shopping spree in Sydney, Australia, where she is in the midst of promoting her latest movie, Challengers. She stopped by vintage shop Irreplaceable, and the store later posted a photo on Instagram of her posing with an employee.

Zendaya embraced spring with her outfit, opting for warm neutrals and easygoing basics. That included a white halter top paired with a creamy pale yellow midi skirt from Ciao Lucia. (Her style is currently sold out, but a similar one is available to shop here.) The skirt featured a ruched drop waist, as well as a stack of bows attached to each side. She finished off the look with a pair of latte-colored Tabi ballet flats by Maison Margiela.

“I always loved clothes and they were in many ways a tool of expression,” Zendaya noted in a 2022 interview. “Fashion has taught me so much about myself and how to be so much more fearless in a lot of different ways. It has also helped me in other avenues of my life, whether it be business or even as an actress, to be more fearless and to not be so concerned with other people’s point of view of what I look like or what I’m wearing.”
