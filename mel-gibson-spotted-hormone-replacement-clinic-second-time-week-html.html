He’s preparing to join forces on the big screen with some of Hollywood’s toughest stars so it’s not surprising Mel Gibson is anxious to bulk up.

On Friday, the 57-year-old actor was spotted at the Beverly Hills Rejuvenation centre, a hormone replacement clinic in Los Angeles, for the second time in less than a week.

It seems Mel is beefing up after winning a role in action film The Expendables 3, which will see him go head-to-head with other action icons such as Sylvester Stallone and Harrison Ford.

Wearing purple striped shorts, a denim shirt, white socks and trainers, the grey haired star looked a far cry from his action hero days.

He was joined by a muscular male pal, who showed off his guns in an all black outfit of tank top, shorts and trainers.

Perhaps he was there to provide some inspiration for Gibson.

Following Mel’s visit to the clinic on Labor Day, it was revealed he completed a session on a high tech pod designed to replicate the effects of high altitude training.

Watch out for those guns: Mel was joined by a friend who showed off his impressive muscles in a black tank top

An onlooker said: 'He had just worked out at a gym and then came straight to the treatment centre.

‘He was grimacing and pulling faces as he crossed the road, so I guess he was getting some of his post-workout aggression out.

‘I was pretty shocked to see him there, but I guess when you get to his age you are not the man you used to be.

‘The first thing he did was spend an hour in the pod. His trainer closed it for him after he climbed in.


He's got some work to do: Mel didn't look as if he was ready to go head-to-head with his fellow action heroes

‘After that he headed came outside and headed into another part of the building, and was inside there for another half-an-hour.’

Meanwhile, last weekend Mel accused police of 'harassing' him during a furious outburst after he was stopped while driving without his licence.

The troubled Oscar-winning actor exploded with rage after being pulled over by Malibu police at a drink-drive checkpoint, according to TMZ.com.

Mel, who had a woman in the passenger seat at the time, erupted when officers asked him to show his driving documents, ranting: 'Why are you harassing me?'

Back in the day: Mel was known for his quickfire delivery and athletic physique 

When asked why he was so angry, Gibson is reported to have said: 'I have had problems with you Lost Hills [Malibu] deputies in the past.'

The Hollywood star was sober, and after his temper cooled, Gibson was let off with a warning for not having the correct documents on him, TMZ.com reports.

The incident is said to have occurred last Saturday, 31 August at 1am on the same stretch of road where he was infamously caught drink-driving in 2006.

A police report filed at the time cited the Braveheart star as cursing: 'F****** Jews... The Jews are responsible for all the wars in the world.'

He allegedly then asked the arresting deputy, 'Are you a Jew?'
