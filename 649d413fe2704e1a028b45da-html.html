NHL Draft 2023 Mock: Predictions for round 2 of this year's draft

August 2024 · 3 minute read

The highly anticipated 2023 NHL Draft is rapidly approaching, bringing with it a wave of excitement and anticipation. In the days leading up to the draft, numerous developments are expected to unfold, potentially reshaping the entire landscape of the event.

Trade discussions are in full swing, teams are engaging in meetings with Matvei Michkov, and speculations and rumors are bound to circulate intensively.

Constructing a mock draft entails a significant amount of speculation and guesswork. Teams tend to be tight-lipped about their preferences and often employ diversionary tactics to mislead others. Attempting to predict the second round of the draft adds another layer of complexity. It's akin to navigating through unpredictable territory.

Given the multitude of variables in play, including the likelihood of impending trades, all we can do at this stage is explore realistic possibilities.
