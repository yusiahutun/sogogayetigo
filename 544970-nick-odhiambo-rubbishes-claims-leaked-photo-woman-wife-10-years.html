Radio presenter Nick Odhiambo has addressed rumours that the leaked photo circulating is of his new wife.

How did Nick Odhiambo's wedding go?

The 43-year-old multi-talented journalist and his longtime lover, Annie Sumbeiywo, tied the knot in a flashy koito (traditional wedding) ceremony.

The lovebirds' flashy wedding was attended by hundreds of guests, who shared photos and videos from their nuptials online.

Nick and Annie dazzled in stylish red outfits made by local designers.

How did Nick react to viral leaked photo?

After their wedding went viral, netizens took to the X app to revive an old screenshot from 2018.

Netizens claimed the lady in the leaked snap was the radio presenter's new wife. The screenshot, however, does not cover the whole conversation, which is misleading.

According to a Kenyans.co.ke article, the full screenshot revealed that Nick did not know the woman and was fooling around with his pals in the chat.

The radio presenter, while addressing the rumours, wrote:

"Mumesema mwanaume hawezi kaa (You all have said a man cannot stay) happily married for more than 24hrs? Who hurt you.. That trending pic is from 10 years ago. And a cropped screenshot. I was wondering why people are calling my number and hanging up all day today."

Machoka lectures people who downplay marriage

In other news, media personality Fred Machoka urged young people who shunned marriages to get partners or risk regretting them in the future.

In a video he shared on his TikTok page, Machoka shaded men and women who downplayed marriage for selfish gains and told them they would not be young forever.

He, however, urged those who are yet to get partners to keep looking, as their situation differed from those who did not desire to get married.

Machoka also sang a TBT song about a woman going through hardships and not finding a suitor because she lived life thinking she was going to be young forever.
