Poldark fans sent into meltdown as Aidan Turner gets semi-naked AGAIN

June 2024 · 6 minute read

For many viewers it was the highlight of the first series – the heady moment when Aidan Turner whipped off his shirt to reveal his chiselled body.

Yet despite baring his chest once again for the opening episode of the second series, Poldark failed to win last night's ratings war – instead being pipped to the post by ITV's Victoria.

Flexing his ripped muscles and showing off his toned torso, Poldark sent 5.1million viewers into a frenzy as he hacked away at rocks with a pickaxe while topless.

But it wasn't quite enough to make viewers stay tuned into the period drama – with 100,000 more opting to watch the less steamy drama about a young Queen Vic.

Official viewing figures show that Victoria was the most watched drama on TV last night - attracting 5.2million viewers on average and a peak audience of 5.9million.

Official viewing figures show that costume drama Victoria (pictured) was the most watched drama on TV last night with 5.2million viewers on average and a peak audience of 5.9million

ITV also attracted a further 7.6million viewers earlier on in the evening with the latest series of the X Factor – an increase of 1.4million viewers compared to last week – making it the most watched show on TV last night.

In contrast, the BBC pulled in 5.1million viewers for the return of Poldark – down on the whopping 9.5million fans that tuned in to watch its first ever episode last March.

However, despite attracting slightly less viewers than Victoria, Poldark fans were still delighted with the return of 33-year-old Turner – who left them wanting more after whipping off his shirt.
