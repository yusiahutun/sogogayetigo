Maurielle Lue Wiki, Husband, Age, Salary, FOX 2, Fired Or Hospitalized?

August 2024 · 5 minute read

The reports had it that Maurielle Lue, the Emmy-award-winning news anchor of Fox 2 Detriot, had been out of the air for a few weeks in 2021. Well, why was that? Was she sick? Was she on a vacation? Did the network fire her? Why has the fan-favorite news anchor been out of the studio?

If you want to know what happened to Maurielle Lue, keep reading. You will also find out other interesting facts about her, like her age, a few details about her husband, her job, etc.

Was Maurielle Lue Fired From FOX 2 Detroit Or Hospitalized?

Maurielle was indeed hospitalized because of COVID-19. But that happened in March/April 2021 and that hospitalization is not the reason behind her absence right now.

In March 2021, on the day after her birthday (read below), Maurielle went to the hospital because she was having trouble breathing and had not been able to eat anything for almost a week. And then she had to spend a week in the hospital.

And even after she returned to her home after a week in the hospital, she continued to have fever and headaches. About the ordeal, Maurielle said, “This is one of the hardest things I’ve ever gone through. The staff and the nurses have been great.” She also thanked her FOX 2 Detriot co-workers.

Now, coming back to the recent absence of Maurielle from Fox 2 Detriot, Maurielle was on a vacation to Bali in October 2021. Towards the end of October, Maurielle had announced that she would be back at the desk in the first week of November.

It is the 8th of November, the first week has already passed, and Maurielle had not yet returned to the show.

On the 1st of November, Maurille had Tweeted that her grandparents were COVID positive. She said that they were already 75 and although they were fully vaccinated, they were still experiencing many symptoms.

On the 5th of November, Maurille again Tweeted that her grandparents were “still fighting the COVID” but they were okay. Her grandparents’ COVID might be the reason for her absence from TV. Hopefully, her grandparents would get better and she would be back in FOX 2 Detriot soon.

Maurielle Lue Husband, Is She Even Married?

The only piece of information about her romantic life comes from an article Seen Magazine ran on her in 2017. During the interview for the article, Maurielle Lue mentioned that she was “dating someone in Detriot” before she moved to the city.

Maurielle said that she was often in the city because of her partner. And slowly, her love for the city of Detriot grew and she settled there. As for the boyfriend, nobody knows what happened to him.

There is no evidence to prove that Maurielle had a husband, or even that she was married.

Maurielle Lue Age

As of November 2021, Maurielle Lue was 36 years of age.

How Much Is Maurielle Lue Salary, Net Worth?

As a news anchor and host for Fox News, Maurille Lue earns about $75 thousand per year, which is the salary of an average Fox News anchor. Her net worth, as of 2021, was roughly about $600 thousand.

Maurielle always wanted to be a part of the “news business”, even when she was in elementary school. She got her first job as a journalist at the age of 15. She was not even out of high school when she worked for Brookwood Upclose in Atlanta.

And at the age of sixteen, Maurielle won her first journalism award for producing a documentary about a local shelter for troubled youth in Atlanta. Later, she attended Hampton University Scripps Howard School of Journalism and Communication and graduated with honors.

Soon after college, Maurille joined NBC station WVVA in West Virginia. Then at the age of 23, she moved to Cleveland to work for WEWS, an affiliate of ABC. Some of the works were featured on ESPN as well. She moved to Detriot after working in Cleveland for about 3 years.

Maurille has been working in Detriot for almost 9 years, and most of those years, she has worked for FOX 2 Detroit. She works as the host of The Nine and morning anchor on the FOX 2 News Team.
