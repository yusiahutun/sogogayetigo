I do... AGAIN! Teresa Palmer weds her husband Mark Webber in legal ceremony at nine months pregnant and seals the deal with a kiss

They got married in an intimate ceremony in Mexico in December.

But Teresa Palmer and her hubby Mark Webber did it all over again on Friday, and this time it was legally binding.

The 27-year-old who is in the final stages of her pregnancy, tweeted: 'Today we were legally married and had a beautiful Christian ceremony led by our pastor @judahsmith Thank you god for the blessings.'

Every bride's dream: Teresa Palmer wed her husband Mark Webber for the second time on Friday in a legally binding ceremony, led by pastor Judah Smith from Seattle's City Church

Spreading the news: The actress announced the news on her Twitter page

Meanwhile her now legal husband, Mark, 33, posted a pic on Instagram with the caption: 'Today I was blessed to be legally married to my beautiful wife @tez_palmer in a special ceremony lead by our amazing pastor @judahsmith and attended by our lovely and supportive friends @chelseasmith @missbrookestone @mariannapalka @kailelion and @richardmelvillehall GOD BLESS!

The pair, who split their time between Adelaide and Los Angeles, were married by Judah Smith from Seattle's City Church in what looked like a modest gathering of close knit friends, including artist Richard Melville Hall aka Moby, and two fluffy pooches.

The beautiful blonde, who is pregnant with her first child, looked glowing as she sat barefooted on the sofa surrounded by her pals, beaming in a elegant, beaded, white gown.

Take two: Teresa looked glowing in her second wedding dress, as the couple's friends joined them for their special day

With her sun kissed blonde locks loose and flowing, she looked happy and relaxed in a very similar dress to the Pallas Couture lace gown she wore on her original wedding day late last year.

Meanwhile, her man wore a simple white T-shirt and nude pants, accessorising with designer stubble and a small pooch.

Later in the day, Teresa also posted a picture of her and husband kissing while holding their two dogs.

She captioned the snap: 'Kiss the ones you love! Even the pups are smooching! Happy (2nd) wedding @likemark happy to be married before God in our Christian wedding with our pastor @judahsmith.'

Earlier this week the Warm Bodies star, clearly reminiscing on their happy day and in anticipation of the next one, also posted a picture of her with her six bridesmaids from December, captioning it: 'To my beautiful bridesmaids. I LOVE you all so much, my soul sisters. Gorgeous dresses on gorgeous bridesmaids all done by @pallascouture forever grateful.'

Christmas bride: Earlier this week the star tweeted a pic of her and her six bridesmaid's from her wedding in December

The pair enjoyed a whirlwind romance after meeting online on Twitter and became engaged while shooting the romantic thriller The Ever After alongside Oscar winner Melissa Leo after 10 months of dating.

Shortly after they announced they were expecting a baby, which is due this month.

The movie-star couple have been keeping busy during the pregancy with Mark promoting his new film Laggis, a black comedy with Keira Knightley, while Teresa is starring in Terence Malick's upcoming Knight of Cups with Cate Blanchett, Natalie Portman and Christian Bale.

Baby bump: The 27-year-old was out and about with her beautiful bump earlier this week, she is due this month
