What causes dark circles?

If your dark circles are hereditary, it’s not possible to get rid of them permanently—which is where a great full-coverage concealer comes in. According to Lolis, dark circles can be caused by thin under-eye skin, hyperpigmentation from chronic irritation, and blood vessels, among other factors. She adds that volume loss around the eyes happens naturally over time, contributing to hollowness and shadows that make dark circles more visible.
