Kim Kardashians Totally See-Through Lace Gown Served Major Goth Glam Energy

February 2024 · 2 minute read

Kim Kardashian has yet to meet an aesthetic she can’t conquer. From rocking girly, Barbiecore-approved gowns to absolutely nailing courtside W.A.G. chic attire, the A-lister has shown she has the range on more than a few occasions — and her latest style showing was no exception.

On Saturday, Kardashian debuted her latest sartorial shift when stepping out at the star-studded Baby2Baby Gala in Los Angeles wearing the epitome of goth glam dressing. For the occasion, the SKIMS founder sported a black mock-neck Balenciaga gown complete with a sheer lace pattern, a waist-cinching black bow, and a dramatic train. Kim layered the totally see-through dress over a tan long-sleeve catsuit, and she accessorized the ensemble with nothing but matching tan pointed-toe heels.

Although Kardashian’s gown may have taken a page out of her sister Kourtney Kardashian’s goth glam stylebook, her makeup — which consisted of a beige lip, rosy cheeks, and a subtle bronze smoky eye — screamed clean girl aesthetic. The mother-of-four rounded out her look by wearing her waist-length brunette hair down in damp waves with a middle part, and she opted for a pearly white manicure to tie the ‘fit together.

Kim’s outing comes amid a busy few weeks for the star, who’s recently been seen hitting hot spots all over New York City. Just last week, the reality star attended the CFDA Fashion Awards in the Big Apple, where she wore not one, but two Chrome Hearts matching sets. While her first look of the evening featured a black maxi skirt paired with a high-neck halter top, she later styled the skirt with a plunging lace-up crop top when heading to a friend’s birthday party that evening.
