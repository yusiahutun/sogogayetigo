Inside my beauty bag video

July 2024 · 3 minute read

India Amarteifio may be best known for portraying the title role of Queen Charlotte in Netflix's Bridgerton spin-off, but the English actress began her career as a child on the West End. Now, at just 22-years-old, she has amassed experience both on screens and red carpets beyond much of her peers. Inevitably, with this comes a unique insight into beauty – specifically, how she utilises products and approaches brand partnerships, she tells Bazaar in this exclusive video interview captured at the Venice Film Festival.

“Deciding which brands to work with is very important to me, to keep one’s integrity,” she explains. “I feel like Armani Beauty is the perfect brand to partner with, especially for Venice, because they champion everything that I stand for” – which is, “enhancing natural beauty rather than trying to manipulate looks”. This, Amarteifio feels, “is great in empowering people to embrace their inner beauty and their natural beauty”.

Iona Wolff

This philosophy can be seen in the products she keeps inside her own beauty bag –though she may have discovered many favourites from the professionals she works with. With make-up, “I like to feel like I’m just an elevated version of myself,” she explains. Her complexion essentials here include the cult classic Giorgio Armani Luminous Silk foundation (“it’s almost as if you’re not wearing foundation”), Chantecaille's Cheek Gelée (“again – almost like a second-skin… but makes me a little bit glowy and rosy”), and the Giorgio Armani Neo Nude A-contour (“to give myself a little bit of depth… because it makes you look more human”).

When it comes to the eyes, Amarteifio takes her lash game seriously. The RevitaLash Advanced Eyelash Conditioner, “an eyelash elixir to help your lashes grow” lays the foundations, then her curler – the Surratt Beauty Relevee Lash Curler – maintain their shape, (“fun fact: I was recommended by my GP to use eyelash curlers because my eyelashes were growing inwards a little bit,” she reveals, “I’ve never looked back!”). Finally, Amarteifio enhances them with the Armani Beauty Ecocentric Mascara, which is “very elongating”, she shares.

Iona Wolff

But make-up has little to do with the actress’ feelings of her own beauty. “I feel most beautiful when I’m confident in myself,” she says. As someone with advancing fame, “I feel like there are days when everything can become quite a lot and feel quite chaotic,” she says, so “when I have routine and my rhythm – I’m fuelling my body right, and my skin’s looking good, my mental health is in check – that’s when I’m feeling most beautiful, inwards and outwards.”

Here, other kinds of beauty products can be of assistance, she says. The SkinCeuticals Silymarin CF serum “helps with blemish control,” she says. “I get quite congested on my chin and my nose so this is my go-to for when troubles occur.” Meanwhile the soothing Aromatherapy Associates Deep Relax Roller Ball, applied to her pressure points, “rebalances me to come back to my ground homeostasis flat point,” Amarteifio expresses.

Discover these and more of the talent’s much-loved beauty buys in her ‘Inside my beauty bag’ video at the top.
