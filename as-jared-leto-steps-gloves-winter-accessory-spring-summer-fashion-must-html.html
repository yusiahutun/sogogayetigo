Gloves give main character energy, so it comes as no surprise that they have been trending with the Hollywood elite for some time. 

For high-octane glamour, stars like Rihanna, Ariana Grande and Salma Hayek have been hitting the red carpet with opera gloves.

Opera gloves were most prevalent as a ladylike fashion accessory in the 1940s to 1960s.

They reach beyond the elbow and traditionally were reserved for formal occasions.

In 2022, anything goes! 

Wear the statement-making accessory to a black-tie event, a night at the opera or the supermarket!  Whether you opt for a long opera glove or a shorter style (think racing gloves), gloves can add a bit of excitement to any look.

Takeaway tip: For a sleek look, opt for a pair of thin gloves with a snug fit around the finger tip. Remove gloves before eating.
