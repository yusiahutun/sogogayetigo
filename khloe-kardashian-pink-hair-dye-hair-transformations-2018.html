Khlo Kardashian's New Baby Pink Hair Is All the Inspiration You Need for a Style Change

July 2024 · 2 minute read

The year 2018 has been incredibly busy for Khloé Kardashian, and it's not over yet. From giving birth to her first child—daughter True Thompson—in April, to fending off a million questions about her love life, the co-founder of Good American literally hasn't stopped.

And now, it looks as though the reality star is ending 2018 in the best way—by undergoing her most epic hair transformation yet. Following in the footsteps of Kim Kardashian West, Khloé has opted to dye her icy blonde hair the most perfect shade of baby pink in time for the holidays.
