Professional Career

Starting her acting journey, Ona Grauer made her acting debut, appearing in a 1996 episode of the TV series “Sliders.” Later, she appeared in two TV series “Breaker High” (1997) and “The Net” (1998) appearing in one episode each.

She was featured in two episodes of the science fiction TV series “First Wave” from 1999 to 2000. During the same time, she made her big-screen debut in the American-British film “Beautiful Joe” playing the role of Ariel.

She appeared in the TV series “John Doe,” “Stargate SG-1,” and “Black Sash” from 2002 to 2003. In 2003, she starred in the German-American-Canadian action horror film “House of the Dead” playing the role of Alicia. Later, she was cast as Katrina in the supernatural drama series 2004 “The Collector.”

She also appeared as Ayiana in an episode of “Stargate Atlantis” that year. Moreover, she was cast as Katarina Weigel in the criminal drama “Intelligence” two years later. Also in 2006, the actress played Sarah Louise in the sci-fi drama “Three Moons Over Milford.”
