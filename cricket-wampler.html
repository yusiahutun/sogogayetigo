Cricket Wampler is an American actress and dancer. She rose to popularity with her role in the Disney+ series Big Shot, where she plays the character ‘Samantha’. Cricket is receiving immense love from this ongoing series. She is also the sister of Philip and Carrie Wampler.

Early Life

Cricket Wampler was born on January 27, 2001, in Tipp City, Ohio. Her birth name was Christina Wampler which was later changed to Cricket Wampler. Her parents are Holly and Chris Wampler and she is the sister of actor Philip Wampler and actress Carrie Wampler. She currently lives in Los Angeles to pursue her acting career. She loves dancing and is a trained dancer and also a specialized in Ballet.

Cricket Wampler Career

Wampler started acting at the age of 8. And her career formed through commercials. She debuted in the entertainment industry through her role in the TV series “Crash & Bernstein” in 2013. The same year she did the role of Rachel in a tv movie ‘Untitled Tad Quill Project’. In 2014, she starred a guest role in the TV series ‘About a Boy, which she is famously known for.

After that, she had an appearance in the tv series ‘Mr. Robinson’. Even though she starred in many tv series and even appeared in many commercials, her fame was with the 2021 Disney original Big Shot. It was her big project, which made her famous. She plays the role of Samantha Finkman, one of the leads, along with big stars like John Stamos, Yvette Nicole Brown, and Jessalyn Gilsig.
