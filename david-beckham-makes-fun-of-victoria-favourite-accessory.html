David Beckham makes fun of Victoria's favourite accessory - but we're obsessed with it

April 2024 · 2 minute read

The footballing legend poked fun at his wife's fashion sense

    Victoria Beckham's cult-adored chain pouch bag is officially back in stock. Great news for accessory aficionados, but what's the fashion editor stamp of approval in comparison to that of a footballing legend? Look out fashionistas, David is coming for you.

    The Spice Girl-turned-designer posted a hilarious video on Instagram on Wednesday in which her husband expressed his deep passion for the bag style.

    Victoria, who often shares outfit videos with her 30.7m followers, was filming in the mirror to deliver the news that her "favourite chain pouch is now back in stock".

    "YES!" David shouted in exaggerated tones from out of shot. Victoria chuckled at her husband's emotional outburst and revealed: "He’s always taking the mickey out of me…"

    READ: Nicola Peltz is an ethereal dream in Victoria Beckham’s favourite outfit

    David took the mickey out of his wife Victoria 

    Victoria's current favourite bag (and David's too apparently) is from her eponymous clothing label. Larger than most clutches, the piece features an envelope design and is inspired by masculine watch straps. The bag, available to shop in black, white and pastel pink in varying sizes, clearly has captured the attention of the former football star as well as the fashion set.

    View post on Instagram 

    "My favourite Chain Pouch is now available in a beautiful white shade, perfect for spring! (@DavidBeckham approves [laughing emoji]) I’m loving styling mine with my new season Tailored Jacket Dress.. coming soon!! xx VB," Victoria captioned her post.

    The fashion designer looked sharp in her navy blazer dress which featured defined shoulder pads and a daring mini length. VB layered the dress over her beloved pantaboots, a hybrid leggings-boots piece of clothing that has become synonymous with the Balenciaga label.

    MORE: Nicola Peltz & Brooklyn Beckham's date night outfits are going to be your new spring uniform

    Her ensemble felt super spring appropriate, the tailored dress was elegant and smart, and yet the white leather of her chain pouch felt evocative of warmer climes. Victoria wore her hair down in loose curls and exuded her trademark polish.

    David has proven himself to be a man of taste, and we are now avidly awaiting his future hot fashion takes…
