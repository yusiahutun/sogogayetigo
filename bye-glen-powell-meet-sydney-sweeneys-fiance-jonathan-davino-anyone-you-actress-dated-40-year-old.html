Jonathan Davino’s family worked in the restaurant business ... or not?

According to reports, Davino’s family worked in the restaurant world. Per Chicago Business, his family owned Mista Pizza and Pompeii Xpress, though these eateries have since closed. People also reports that he is the owner of a couple of vaporiser packaging companies like Final Bell and 14th Round.

However, Sweeney herself recently refuted this information in an interview with Glamour UK, stating: “He’s not a restaurateur. I have no idea where that came from. He’s also not the heir of a pizza company. He’s a business guy. He’s from Chicago. We’ve been watching [this narrative] for six years now and I’m like, ‘What the hell?’”

He works with Sweeney in the film biz

According to Grazia, Sweeney launched her production company Fifty-Fifty Films in 2020. She’s since landed partnerships with Jean-Marc Vallée and Nathan Ross’ Crazyrose and Endeavor Content, and worked on HBO Max’s The Players Table, starring herself and Halsey.

Are Sydney Sweeney and Glen Powell together – or just friends?

While their on- and off-screen chemistry is undeniable, People recently confirmed that Sweeney and Powell are just friends after all. Sweeney is still engaged to her partner, of course, while Powell is currently single.

But, as the saying goes, any press is good press, and the reports about their rumoured romance helped draw more eyeballs to their film. “I mean, we sold a romcom,” Sweeney told People. “We were just filming a fun, amazing movie.”

In her December cover story for Glamour UK, Sweeney suggested the rumours may have started because she’s rarely photographed with her fiancé. She added that the rumours were “really hard on Glen”, saying that “it made me sad … because [otherwise] it was such a beautiful experience for the both of us. I care for him so much”.
