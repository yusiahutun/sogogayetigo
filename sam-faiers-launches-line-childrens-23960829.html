Sam Faiers launches line of children's body and haircare products exclusive to Tesco

February 2024 · 4 minute read

Bath time should be a relaxing time, and if you're looking for some affordable goodies to treat your little one to a splashing time, there's an exciting new launch from celebrity mum Samantha Faiers to try out.

The ex-TOWIE member and Mummy Diaries Star has launched a children’s body and haircare range called Knightley’s Adventures and it's available exclusively in Tesco stores and online at Tesco.com now.

The safari-inspired features different products, each item in the range retails at £3 and is named after the surname of her partner Paul Knightley and their children Paul, 5, and Rosie, 3.

Knightley's Adventures includes a host of children’s bath time essentials, from body wash and bubble bath to bath bombs and de-tangling spray. The range is designed to take children on a bath-time safari adventure and to inspire their imaginations with delicious scents, interesting facts and hand drawn illustrations adorning each product.

All products in the range are designed for sensitive skin, approved by a paediatrician and tested by a skin expert, as well as being suitable for vegans.

Paula Brady, Category Buying Manager at Tesco says, “We’re excited to bring a new, vibrant range of children’s toiletries exclusively to our customers. This is our biggest ever range of children’s toiletries created by a celebrity mum, and we know shoppers will be looking forward to trying out the whole range.”

We've listed all of the products below for your convenience.
