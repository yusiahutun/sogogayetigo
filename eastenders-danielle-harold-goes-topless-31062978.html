EastEnders Danielle Harold goes topless as robe falls down leaving fans in a frenzy

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Former EastEnders star Danielle Harold set pulses racing yet again after she went topless for a racy snap.

The actress, who played Lola Pearce, is never shy to share a sizzling social media post and show off her jaw-dropping figure. And last week Danielle was back it – and she soon left her fans wanting more.

Taking to her Instagram, the 31-year-old looked nothing short of sensational in a plunging white robe that showed off her bare chest underneath. The beauty was getting ready for a glam night out as she casually prepared for her big event.

As expected, fans were left gushing over Danielle’s racy BTS snap. Rushing to the comments section, one said: “So beautiful.”

Echoing their thoughts, someone else proclaimed: “And people wonder why Danielle is my celebrity crush!” A third also quipped: “You absolute beauty.”

Another follower then chimed in and said: “Wow smoking hot.” While a fifth penned: “You’re on another level.”

It comes after EastEnders bagged a ton of gongs at this year’s Inside Soap Awards - including Danielle. She won Best Actress and her on-screen husband Jamie Borthwick, who plays Jay Brown, was crowned Best Actor. The whole cast also got a piece of the action after scooping Best Soap.

The storyline based around Lola's cancer diagnosis and tragic death won Best Storyline and Danielle's on-screen daughter Isabella Brown, who plays Lexi Pearce, went home with the Best Young Performer award. Lexi's on-screen dad Max Bowden, who plays Ben Mitchell, was crowned Soap Superstar of 2023.

Coronation Street went home with four gongs as Maureen Lipman was awarded Best Comic Performance for the third year in a row for her role as acid-tongued Evelyn Plummer.
