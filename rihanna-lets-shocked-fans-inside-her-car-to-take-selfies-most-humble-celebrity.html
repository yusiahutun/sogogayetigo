Rihanna lets shocked fans inside her car to take selfies - 'most humble celebrity'

March 2024 · 2 minute read

Rihanna has shown she’s a humble queen as she went one step further than taking selfies with her fans, she let them inside her car! She’s even made Beyoncé and Taylor Swift fans jealous…

The music star has had an extremely successful career spanning over two decades, and even though she hasn’t released an album in eight years, Rihanna still has one of the most loyal fanbases around. It’s no wonder after she treats them like her besties!

Rihanna lets fans into her car and Beyoncé and Taylor Swift fans are jealous

During her recent visit to Milan, fans caught the star in the car, and like any fan would, asked her for a picture. However, to their surprise, instead of getting out herself, she let them in the SUV.

The fans began knocking on the door asking for a picture, as they slid open, allowing the fans to go inside to take a picture with their idol.

When the fans exited after getting the snap that’s going to slay their Instagram feed, the Rude Boy singer blew them a kiss and thanked them, before telling them to “be safe.”

Of course, fans on X, formerly known as Twitter went wild over the rare interaction, wishing it could be them with their faves. Us too!

“The amount of trust she has is admirable,” wrote one.

“Beyonce take notes! That‘s when we‘re going to buy your product,” wrote one fan.

One Swiftie went as far as to say: “This could be Taylor Swift but literally none of us have any chill at all.”

Another joked: “Personally……. I wouldn’t get out of the car until she released R9 that’s just me though.”

A fourth said: “The sweetest most humble celebrity. I love her.”

No word on the album just yet – but A$AP drops hints

As fans ask why the ‘car fans’ didn’t ask about when her new album is dropping, others joke that there were “terms and conditions,” that you were only allowed in if you didn’t ask about the album.

However, her partner A$AP Rocky recently hinted ‘something was coming’ when he ran into fans a couple of weeks ago.

“She’s working on it,” he told keen fans, as they asked when they would be getting album number nine from RiRi.

However, fans who had been waiting eight long years were hesitant to believe him, so I guess we’ll just have to wait and see. As if we haven’t been doing enough waiting…
