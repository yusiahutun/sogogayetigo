About

Karen Grassle is a 82 year old American Actress. Born Karen Trust Grassle on 25th February, 1942 in Berkeley, California, USA, she is famous for her roles as Caroline "Ma" Ingalls in the NBC Western historical drama series Little House on the Prairie, Fran in the CBS Western drama series Gunsmoke, Kathy in the 1981 comedy-drama film Harry’s War, Ellen Foster Holland in the 1985 NBC drama TV film Between the Darkness and the Dawn, Mrs. Sutherland in the 1994 biographical Western film Wyatt Earp, Aunt Dorothy in the 2012 biographical drama film My Greatest Teacher, the mysterious woman in the 2017 horror mystery thriller short film Where’s Roman?, and Melody in the 2021 drama film Not to Forget in a career that spans 1966–present.
