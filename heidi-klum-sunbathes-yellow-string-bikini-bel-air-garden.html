Bronzed Heidi Klum sizzles in her backyard in tiny string bikini

January 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Heidi Klum set pulses racing on Saturday as she shared a sun-drenched snap lounging in the garden of her stunning $9.8 million Bel-Air mansion whilst rocking a sunshine-yellow string bikini. 

The America's Got Talent judge, 49, rarely shies away from a daring fashion moment, be it her enviable archive of modelsque swimwear or her scene-stealing red carpet looks. Heidi didn't miss an opportunity to strike a pose in her yellow ribbed bikini, whilst also donning a fur bucket hat and golden eye treatment masks.

"Finally some sunshine," the Germany's Next Top Model judge captioned her series of snaps, which caused a flurry of comments from her doting fans.

 "Looking incredible Heidi," penned one fan, as another wrote: "You are my sunshine." 

"Always be my favourite model," added a third, while a fourth chimed in: "You always look so beautiful Heidi!" 

In an interview with Women's Health last year, the former Victoria's Secret model credited garden workouts as her favourite way to stay in shape. The mother-of-four's epic home, which she shares with her husband Tom Kaulitz, also features an immaculate high-tech gym.

The property boasts incredible hilltop views and a massive swimming pool which has been showcased on Heidi's Instagram feed. But as well as providing a beautiful backdrop for social media posts to her 7.9 million-strong following, her spectacular backyard is also her favourite play to workout. 

She explained: "I'm fortunate enough to have a pool at home and, when the pandemic started, I began swimming 20 laps a day." It is not only her physical fitness that is enhanced by her glorious outdoor space, but also her inner wellbeing as she likes to eat homegrown vegetables planted from her very own garden. 

She revealed: "I've started a little vegetable patch in my garden. I have fun with the kids making squash muffins. It's good for them to see healthy meals made from scratch."
