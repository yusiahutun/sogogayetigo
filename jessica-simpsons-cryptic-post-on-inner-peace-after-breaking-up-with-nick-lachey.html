Jessica Simpson's cryptic post on 'inner peace' after breaking up with Nick Lachey

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Jessica Simpson has taken to her Instagram to share her first ‘Leica’ selfie taken in 2007, as she reflects on what she would have told her 27-year-old self at the time.

At the time of the photo, the music star would have just divorced from her first husband, Nick Lachey. The couple had their own TV show, Newlyweds which ran from 2003-2005, ending just a year before they filed for divorce.

Jessica Simpson reveals what she would have told 27-year-old self

Taking to her 6.3 million Instagram followers, Simpson posted a throwback selfie of her younger self, a year after breaking up with Nick Lachey, as she can be seen staring off into the distance on her balcony.

In the caption, the 43-year-old penned:

“My very first Leica selfie in 2007. The first thought that comes to mind is ‘insomniac’ and the second thought would be to tell my 27-year-old self ‘thank you for staying awake to listen to your dreams inside the glow. The soul studies are what produce character, moral clarity, and inner peace’.

“When we have a harsh thought of judgment upon first sight, skip to the second thought with compassion and appreciation for ourselves,” she concluded.

Fans are ‘inspired’ by the star

After vulnerably opening up to her fans, they then did the same and told Jessica how she had inspired them over the years.

“If only your 27-year-old self knew how much you were saving 17-year-old me & many others with your art. I was essentially homeless, sleeping on my friends’ couch at 17 while my parents battled their addictions. You brought me so much positivity and fun through your music, movies & commercials. It was a joy to root for you and in a way, myself, to push through the noise and embrace the positive,” shared one follower.

“I remember this part of your book when you talked about your artistic phase so it’s so cool to see u post photos from the time <3 Love u,” wrote another.

Another inspired fan penned: “I wish you could read this so I can thank you for being such great inspiration and motivation for so many years, Life has been such a journey and you have helped along the way!”

Jessica Simpson opens up on changes to her body

Being in the spotlight for as many as she has, of course, anyone is going to go through changes. One prominent change in Jessica’s body that fans noticed, was her weight loss over the past couple of years, which sparked from a viral ad.

Rumors about the Irresistible singer using Ozempic began to spread, however, she recently shut these rumors down in an interview with Bustle.

Speaking on how she actually lost weight, Jessica revealed it actually wasn’t the diabetes drug, in fact, it was willpower.

“Oh Lord. I mean, it is not [Ozempic] – it’s willpower. I’m like, do people want me to be drinking again? Because that’s when I was heavier. Or they want me to be having another baby? My body can’t do it,” she told the publication.
