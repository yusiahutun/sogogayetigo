Bucket List Ideas for March 2019

June 2024 · 2 minute read

.

After a never-ending January and a very snowy February, we are so ready for spring. March has finally arrived, and with it comes the promise of more sunshine (thank you, Daylight Saving Time), porch-sitting weather, and the chance to beautify your backyard. Celebrate spring's arrival this month by trying something new. Whether you plan a trip, get back your green thumb, or just celebrate St. Patrick's Day, there are plenty of ways to bid winter farewell.

Click through our ideas, tips, and favorite products for the ultimate March below. Then, pin or print the master list so you can check things off throughout the month.
