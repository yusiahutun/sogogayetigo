Bowl predictions: The Athletic staff picks all 41 college football bowl games

May 2024 · 3 minute read

The second half of college football bowl season is underway, with the week after Christmas jam-packed all the way to New Year’s Day, with the College Football Playoff semifinals set to be played in the late afternoon on January 1.

There are 24 games between December 26 and January 1 plus the College Football Playoff national championship game on January 8.

Advertisement

Pick ’em pools are commonplace during bowl season and we’re doing our own staff version here. Seven staff members at The Athletic made their straight up picks for all 41 bowl games.

There are seven unanimous picks: Jacksonville State over Louisiana in the New Orleans Bowl, Louisville to beat USC in the Holiday Bowl, SMU to top Boston College in the Fenway Bowl, LSU over Wisconsin in the ReliaQuest Bowl and Oregon to knock off Liberty in the Fiesta Bowl.

As for the College Football Playoff games, five of our seven pickers are going with Michigan to beat Alabama while four took Texas to beat Washington.
