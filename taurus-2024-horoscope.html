Taurus 2024 Horoscope: A Total Personality Shift

June 2024 · 4 minute read

This year, you may very well be coming into eminence, because your Taurus horoscope for 2024 is primed for stardom. You’re also tapping into your ability to take control over your career, your image, and your level of authority both in—and out—of work.

Because you were born during this era, you’re destined to not only succeed at your chosen field, but transform it all together. You’ll begin to get a sense of what you’ll accomplish after transformative Pluto in Aquarius reactivates your 10th house of publicity from January 20 to September 1, as well as after November 19, which will drive you to project a personal brand that exudes power. Whether it’s proving everyone who ever doubted you wrong or simply fulfilling a dream you’ve had since you were a child, you’re tapping into what motivates you to win. However, before you start making waves, you may need to heal and let go of all the fears and negative mindsets that sabotage you once the North Node of Destiny joins forces with Chiron in your 12th house of subconscious programming on February 19.

By the time a lunar eclipse in Libra rises in your sixth house of work and health on March 25, you’ll put a plan in place that helps you practice your craft and maintain your self-care in a way that leads you to success. You may even get a new pet that acts as a loving companion throughout your day-to-day life, as the sixth house also rules over service animals! However, when a total solar eclipse casts a shadow over your spiritual 12th house on April 8, you’ll experience a shift that unfolds deep within; a shift that may activate your psychic abilities and allow you to forgive yourself for the unrealistic standards you failed to meet.

The most exciting moment of 2024 arrives on April 20, when Jupiter joins forces withUranus in Taurus, bringing luck, prosperity, and innovation to your first house of the self. This could lead to a total personality shift, prompting you to embrace changes in your appearance and identity. People who have known you for years may notice that you’re suddenly acting “different,” but that’s only because you’re embracing your true self. And if they don’t like it, there’s a good chance it’s because you’re no longer bowing to their expectations. Venus—your ruling planet—enters Taurus on April 29, where it will increase your desire to treat yourself, take care of yourself, and make yourself feel glamorous. After you’ve embraced the new and improved you, Jupiter enters your second house of finances and self-worth on May 25, where it will attract abundance and strengthen your bank account for the remainder of the year. Take advantage of this energy by doing money spells and raking in that extra dough.

On September 17, a lunar eclipse in Pisces rises in your 11th house of community, hopes, and dreams, which could jumpstart a meteoric rise to notoriety. Not only are you making all the right connections, but you’re further positioning yourself create the world you’ve always wanted to live in. But as a solar eclipse in Libra lands in your heavy-lifting sixth house on October 6, you’ll realize just how much work it’s gonna take to get where you want to be.

As the year comes to a close, you may run into conflicts at home as aggressive Mars retrogrades through your fourth house of family and ancestral roots as of December 6. This will not only force you to fix that leaky faucet and clean our your cluttered closet, but it will also encourage you to finally “have it out” with that relative you’ve been holding a grudge against for centuries. Let it all out—in a constructive way, of course—because it’s the only way you’ll be able to strengthen your sense of security and comfort for the long haul.
