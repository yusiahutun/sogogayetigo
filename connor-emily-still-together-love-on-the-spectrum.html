Are Connor, Emily Still Together From Love on the Spectrum Season 2? Where Are They Now?

April 2024 · 3 minute read

Adorable dates. Here’s if Connor and Emily are still together after Love On The Spectrum season 2.

Creator Cian O’Clery described how they made the show on Netflix to Tudum. He mentioned that he met a lot of people who had autism while he was working with Love on the Spectrum co-executive producer Karina Holden. “Not only did we get to know the people we were filming really well, but we also spoke to hundreds and hundreds of people on the spectrum about their lives and what was important to them. The story of people wanting to find love and wanting to date just kept coming up. To us, that was something that wasn’t right. There was obviously a lack of support, and it just felt like a really interesting area to shine a light on.”

He continued, “The other thing that we noticed was the diversity of the spectrum and just how different people can be. That’s the most important thing that we can do with this series, to show that huge diversity that people don’t really understand. I should note, as well, it’s really important to acknowledge that not everybody who is on the spectrum needs support in the world of dating and relationships. Everyone’s so different. But a lot of people do, and a lot of people we spoke to did and were really calling out for that.”

Connor is a newcomer to season 2. He has fears of living the “rest of [his] life alone” and stepped up to the plate and out of his comfort zone. He met Emily at a speed dating event and initially rejected her, but started to have feelings for her.

“I met this girl at the speed dating event but I marked her as a no because she wasn’t really into nature.” Conner’s mother asked if he regretted his decision to which he agreed and said, “I’m beginning to think maybe I was too hasty.”

Are Connor and Emily still together after Love On The Spectrum?

Connor and Emily are still together and it looks like he’s mustering up the courage to ask her to be his girlfriend. Connor is active on social media, but hasn’t posted anything about their relationship.

In one of the last moments together in the show, Emily confessed her feelings, “I hope you don’t think me blunt or anything but I kind of like you.”

Connor still seems to enjoy walking his dogs with his mom as he posted on Instagram a couple of days before season 2’s premiere.
