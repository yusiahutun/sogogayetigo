The Denim Style Celebrities Wear Is on October Prime Day Sale at Amazon

July 2024 · 3 minute read

We’re in the season of denim: maxi skirts, oversized jackets, chambray tops, and, of course, jeans. And while it often feels like there’s an endless list of styles you could pick from (talk about overwhelming), celebrities have really only been gravitating toward one. From Jennifer Lopez to Eva Longoria and Katie Holmes, everyone has quietly decided this year is all about wide-leg jeans. 

As someone who finds the billowing denim style to be the most flattering — because nothing gives the illusion of a cinched waist more than a totally oversized leg — I’m not complaining. So during Amazon’s Prime Big Deal Days, I’m making sure to grab a few more pairs of the denim trend that looks just as good with a simple T-shirt and loafers as it does an oversized button-down and heels while a number of shopper-favorite styles are on sale.

Shop On-Sale Wide-Leg Jeans:

Grapent Wide-Leg Baggy Jeans

Grapent’s Wide-Leg Baggy Jeans are a favorite among Amazon shoppers, with more than 1,300 five-star ratings. This style is available in 16 washes, including classic blues and vibrant pops of color, and is made with a stretchy cotton blend that customers say “allows them to fit well in all the places [they] usually struggle with, including tummy control.” And one fan who explained that they often struggle to find jeans that fit their curvy figure wrote that they “love these,” adding that there’s “just enough stretch to be active…and not be uncomfortable.” “[They’re] the most comfortable and flattering jeans I own,” raved another person.

Levi's 94 Baggy Wide-Leg Jeans

Jennifer Aniston and I agree, Levi’s is that denim brand, and right now, its 94 Baggy Wide-Leg Jeans are 43 percent off. Another shopper was impressed with the 100-percent cotton fabrication, saying that the jeans “feel like good quality and look [like] it,” noting that they get “a lot of compliments” when they wear the wide-leg style.

Gap High-Rise Wide-Leg Jeans

Gap’s 100-percent cotton jeans are now just $48, and according to customers, they are “super flattering” and “comfortable.” Shoppers say that the “fit is spot-on” and the material is “soft.” Plus, they’re incredibly stylish: “These can be dressed up or down,” wrote one fan of the jean’s versatility, while another person raved that the “perfect” style gets them “lots of compliments.”

Wide-leg jeans are the most versatile, flattering pants you can have in your fall wardrobe, and right now, they’re majorly on sale at Amazon. Shop more styles from brands including Joe’s Jeans and Mother — a Taylor Swift go-to.
