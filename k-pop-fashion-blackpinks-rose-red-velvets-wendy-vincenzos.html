Last weekend, Red Velvet’s Wendy dropped her video for Like Water from her first mini-album. She wore a peachy pink asymmetrical ruffled number by Giambattista Valli, a lace midi dress by Australian designer Zimmermann, and a lilac lace maxi by RTW brand Self-Portrait. 

K-pop diets revealed: how Blackpink’s Jennie, BTS’ V and IU stay in shape

Last week, Blackpink style icon Rosé launched the MV for her second solo song, Gone. Rosé was in her usual bows and cute dresses, perfectly contrasted by touches of denim, lightweight knits and her signature chunky boots. Our faves: her soft lilac gingham dress from Thai label TandT, the rainbow feathered dress by Vietnamese designer Nguyen Cong Tri, and an electric blue David Koma jacket, worn with combat boots by Darker Wavs.
In March, K-pop sweetheart IU released her spring offering, Lilac, featuring granny chic and vintage looks with a fun, feminine and modern twist. Key outfits include a preppy plaid coat with a pussy-bow blouse and a knit vest layered over a retro-inspired, all-purple outfit featuring a wool tweed skirt by Gucci and an oriental-style dress covered in red flowers by Alice + Olivia. 

K-drama’s Byun Yo-han is back with The Book of Fish

Two ways to elegance: K-drama actresses Ha Ji-won and Seo Ye-ji step out in chic looks that best convey their personal style

Veteran actress of Secret Garden fame Ha Ji-won attended the press event for her upcoming series, Dramaworld 2, and was a picture of elegance. She wore her hair down and a simple white suit by Etro, a pair of strappy heels by Stuart Weitzman, and an emerald green heart-shaped necklace.Seo Ye-ji, 2020’s breakout star, graced the press event for her mystery-thriller film Recalled clad in Dior. Wearing soft greys and dainty nudes, she looked as ethereal as ever in a ruffled frock with a Victorian neckline and long sleeves. The look was elevated by a pair of Dior Rose des Vents earrings and a Dior double belt.

Bag their style: Vincenzo’s Jeon Yeo-bin and Penthouse 2’s Lee Ji-ah carry the latest “It” totes

Jeon Yeo-bin, who has mastered the art of power dressing for her role in Vincenzo as vicious lawyer Hong Cha-young, has been spotted toting around bags by Fendi, Givenchy, Alexander McQueen, Salvatore Ferragamo, Marni and Joy Gryson. Meanwhile in Korean drama Penthouse 2, Lee Ji-ah showed off the latest Ports 1961 handbag.
