10 Cute Crop Top Outfits to Borrow from Celebs ASAP

February 2024 · 5 minute read

It's unclear what parts of the '90s and early aughts haven't taken over the fashion industry yet, but one thing has remained true — they don't seem to be going away anytime soon. It's even more unclear why we've allowed some of the most questionable clothing trends in past decades, like low-rise bottoms, back into our closets. Regardless of how you feel toward hip huggers, though, their sister trend has received a more warm welcome. Crop top outfits have had less controversy around their recirculation than the 2000s-era whale tale.

Even if you're skeptical of the return of crop top outfits, however, you'll be happy to know crop tops have (thankfully) evolved over the years. Plus, with styling hacks like tucking the hem of your shirt into your bra, strategically tucking and snapping your bodysuit, or maneuvering your tank top straps to create a ruched style, it's never been easier to create a crop top outfit that best suits your aesthetic. Not to mention, there are countless examples of crop top outfits floating around should you need inspiration. Although we love the looks influencers are putting together across Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest, celebrities have also been expertly sporting crop tops both on and off the clock.

Ahead, we've rounded up 10 of our favorite crop top outfits from off-duty celebs that can easily be recreated with pieces already in your closet.

Make It Monochromatic

If you love a matching moment, you’ll love the way Vanessa Hudgens styled her cropped turtleneck tank. The Downtown Owl actress paired the top with a matching mini skirt, chunky black heels, and a leather-style blazer jacket. The only details not adhering to the all-black-everything ensemble? The hardware on her purse, earrings, and, of course, her massive diamond engagement ring. 

At the Ballgame 

Megan Thee Stallion's crop top outfit proves even the most passionate sports fans — who'd never attend a game wearing anything but their favorite jersey — can get in on this trend. To make her team's typically bulky merch into a crop top, the rapper simply unbuttoned the front of her jersey and, using the ends, tied a knot right below the center of her chest. She completed the look with matching high-rise jeans and a clean pair of sneakers, accessorizing with a chunky necklace stack.

As Athleisure

Olivia Wilde took the athleisure route with her crop top outfit, opting for a cropped hoodie instead of a traditional tee. You could pair the lax top with practically anything — jeans, shorts, yoga pants, a miniskirt — but seeing as the Don't Worry Darling director was visiting the gym, she opted for a pair of leggings, running shoes, and a high ponytail. 

Opt for a Cropped Vest

Cropped sweater vests exude cool grandpa vibes. They are simultaneously cute and comfortable and offer a vintage feel even if you just bought them off the rack. Clearly, Hailey Bieber concurs, and her effortless masculine-meets-feminine street style shines through here as she layers the grey V-neck over a cropped white baby tee and pairs them with denim Bermuda shorts and a leather duster. Accessories, including a small handbag and statement earrings, soften the crop top outfit.

Lean Into the Preppy Look

Rihanna's maternity looks were on point when she was pregnant with her first, so it's no surprise that she wouldn't sacrifice her style for baby No. 2 either. The singer stepped out proudly baring her belly in a striped Loewe polo top she made into a crop top via the bra-tuck method, pairing the top with baggy, low-rise jeans, chunky gold jewels, and a square handbag.  

Mix Business with Pleasure

Gigi Hadid also wore a monochromatic crop top outfit while appearing on Good Morning America — but this time, the combination of a crop top and pencil skirt caught our attention. The model-turned-designer's skirt does boast a slit, but you could opt for a more modest pencil skirt and lean into the contrast of a more conservative bottom with the skin-baring top, and it would have the same effect. 

As a Pop of Color

Ashley Park’s crop top outfit is full of surprises. The actress wore a hot pink bustier top underneath a smart pinstripe suit while filming Only Murders in the Building. It's unclear whether the crop top outfit was her off-duty look or part of her character's wardrobe, but either way, we can't wait to copy it piece for piece.

Go Dressy-Casual

Ashley Roberts has come a long way since her days as a Pussycat Doll, but clearly, the performer still loves a crop top outfit. Similar to Park, Roberts's look strikes the perfect balance of dressed up and dressed down: Her crop top and high-waisted shorts combo appears light and airy, but she pairs the set with white stilettos, a mini handbag, and a high bun. It's a going-out outfit worth adding to your mood board.

Stick to a Color Family

Rather than sticking with one color, try keeping to a color palette the way Lana Condor does here with her crop top outfit. The actress leans into the neutral look with shades of cream and beige, but you could copy this outfit format with any hues you'd like.

Simplify the Style

Last but certainly not least, if your ideal crop top outfit is more low-key, look to Kristin Cavallari for inspiration. The business mogul and cookbook author recently stepped out in New York City wearing a basic but classic combo: a crisp, white crop top, distressed blue jeans, and white sneakers. It doesn't get much simpler than that, and you can accessorize as you please or not at all.
