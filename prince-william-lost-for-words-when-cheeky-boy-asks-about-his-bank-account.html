Prince William lost for words when cheeky boy asks about his bank account

January 2024 · 2 minute read

You can always count on children to ask what adults are thinking but don’t dare to say, which is exactly what happened when one cheeky chap asked Prince William how much was in his bank account.

Prince William was on a royal visit to Manchester to learn about a project tackling youth violence, but it seemed like one young chap wanted to learn more about William than he was willing to divulge.

Prince William ‘answers question’ about his bank account

The children on Manchester weren’t holding back when they had an opportunity to speak to the heir to the throne.

One young boy, Amir Hassan had him chuckling and lost for words when he asked: “How much do you have in your bank account?” 

Although we’re all desperate for the answer, the 41-year-old revealed that he didn’t even know the answer himself.

The Prince, who attended the school without Kate Middleton, seemed to be in a cheeky and humorous mood himself on the visit, as he was also asked by students if he wanted to join their art project.

Ironically, the project was about hairstyles the children thought were empowering and positive. Ever the joker, the Prince replied: “I’m literally the last person you should ask. My hair is disappearing.”

However, it doesn’t seem like his hair disappearing is a problem, as William was recently crowned the world’s hottest bald man.

William ‘can’t see a way out’ of rift with Harry

Despite his jokey nature, it looks like William won’t be joking around with his brother, Prince Harry anytime soon.

With Christmas fast approaching, friends of the Sussexes claimed to the Sunday Times that Harry and Meghan Markle would “readily accept” a Christmas invitation to Sandringham to spend it with the rest of the royal family. However, the source says there have been no invitations as of yet.

BBC royal correspondent Jennie Bond has now claimed Wills “won’t be shedding a tear over this” although, he still loves his brother.

“I don’t think William will be shedding a tear over this. Deep down, I’m sure he still loves Harry, but I don’t think he can see a way out of the rift that has opened between them,” she told The Sun.

She then said she thought William had “closed his mind to the prospect of reconciliation with Harry, and I can’t see a time when Meghan would be welcomed back into the bosom of the family.”
