Bunchie Young college offers

The 15-year-old budding athlete has caught the interest of college recruiters. He has been seen by millions of people worldwide in various capacities, including the Super Bowl commercial and numerous videos online showing his remarkable athleticism.

He received a scholarship offer from the University of Illinois to play football in 2017 for the Fighting Illini. The news of the scholarship offer opened a larger conversation around schools offering young kids scholarships at a young age.

The most interesting thing is that Bunchie Young wouldn’t be eligible to play for the Illini until 2025. According to NCAA (National Collegiate Athletic Association) rules, learning institutions cannot offer children written offers until August of the player's senior season. However, this doesn't apply to verbal offers.
