Southern Charm's Kathryn Dennis has admitted she had an abortion while with ex Thomas Ravenel, but clarified it was before she entered rehab, DailyMail.com can reveal. 

Dennis fired back at the father of her two children, Kensie, 5, and Saint, 3, in the latest filing of their explosive custody battle, weeks after Ravenel accused her of having an alcohol, prescription drug and shopping problem. 

Dennis, whose mother Allison Calhoun Dennis died aged 59 on Wednesday, began dating 56-year-old Ravenel in 2013. 

The 27-year-old writes in her affidavit that she learned she became pregnant with a third child by Ravenel in early 2016. At the time, the on-off couple had briefly reconciled following the birth of their son Saint in November of 2015.

But their relationship broke down again, with Dennis stating: 'This time, I did not believe that Thomas and I would ever get back together... I didn't think we could handle a third child. Accordingly, I had an abortion in February of 2016.'

The revelation comes weeks after Ravenel's legal team claimed Dennis entered rehab to hide having an abortion, as Dennis battles for sole custody after Ravenel was accused of sexual assault by the couple's former nanny, Dawn Ledwell.

However, Dennis does admit to taking doctor-prescribed prescription drugs and taking CBD oil products, but claims she has since quit CBD products.  

Dennis admits to not handling her finances well, writing in her affidavit: 'I freely admit to lacking knowledge in regard to my financial matters.

'Accordingly, I have hired Elliot and Davis CPAs to help me with preparing, filing and paying what is currently owed for prior tax returns. 

'I estimate that I will easily owe in the range of $100,0000 - $150,000 of back taxes when I get prior tax returns filed in the foreseeable future.' 

In July, Ravenel filed legal papers that accused the mother of his children of being addicted to prescription medications, marijuana, alcohol, shopping and sex. 

The filings were an attempt to show Dennis as a negligent mother, mentally unstable, heavily medicated, sexually active with multiple men and hopelessly reckless with her finances.
