Top 10 highest-paid voice actors in Japan in 2022 (seiyuus)

June 2024 · 6 minute read

Did you know that seiyuu means voice actor in Japanese? Voice acting has existed for many years now. In Japan, the growing popularity of anime series and movies led to increasing demand for seiyuus. The highest-paid voice actors in the country are known for voicing various characters effortlessly and with passion.

It has taken years for the highest-paid voice actors in Japan to perfect their craft. Today, they can voice different characters and narrate for various types of media such as anime, audio dramas, video games, live-action stunts, commercials, and puppet shows.

Highest-paid voice actors in Japan in 2022

The highest-paid seiyuu pockets millions of dollars every year. They work hard and have starred in various anime productions, commercials, video games, puppet shows, and more.
