Josh Flagg Bio, Net Worth, Married, Husband, and Siblings

February 2024 · 3 minute read

One of the famous Real Estate agent in America as well as a Television personality Joshua Daniel Flagg is also known as Josh Flagg. He is famous for his TV show titled Million Dollar Listing in Los Angeles.

Los Angeles, California born Josh Flagg is also the host of The Million Dollar Man and real estate agent of The Altman Brothers. In 2015, Flagg featured on Forbes’ 30 Under 30 list.

Josh Flagg Bio (Siblings)

Josh Flagg was born on August 20, 1985, to parents Michael and Cindy Flagg. He is the grandson of Herman Platt– an American business executive and Jewish Leader. He grew up in Los Angeles and completed his high school graduation from Beverly Hills High School.

Flagg started working as a real estate agent at the age of 18. He made residential sales in high ends neighborhoods like Beverly Hills and the Sunset Strip. Throughout his career as a real estate agent, he has set many records sales.

In 2006, Flagg along with Chad Rogers and Madison Hildebrand started a new reality TV show- Million Dolar Listing Los Angeles.  He released his first book, A Simple Girl: Stories My Grandmother Told Me in 2009 and the second book, Million Dollar Agent: Broking the Dream in 2011. He also has launched his mobile app named ‘Star Maps’ in 2012.

Josh Flagg Net Worth

Josh Flagg accumulated a huge sum of money from his career as a real estate agent and media personality. As of 2018, he has an estimated net worth of $25 million. Furthermore, he has sold more than $1 billion worth of property which also includes the highest sale of Brentwood Park and a $25 million residential sales in Beverly Hills.

Besides all these, Josh makes thousands of dollars from his Television Shows and millions of dollars from his company. He owns a penthouse in the Century Towers complex, Century City and an estate of Michael Filerman.
