23 best dressed celebrities at the 2023 NTAs: Holly Willoughby, Rochelle Humes &amp; more

February 2024 · 5 minute read

Discover the most memorable fashion from the National Television Awards 2023 at London's O2 Arena

    It's that time of year again – the National Television Awards are back! The great and the good of British showbusiness have descended upon London's O2 Arena to put on a stunning sartorial display at the annual awards ceremony, which celebrates the best television from the past year.

    While we can't wait to see our favourite shows including Strictly Come Dancing, Happy Valley and Love Island battle it out and watch Ant McPartlin and Declan Donnelly secure the Best Presenter gong for the 22nd year running, it's the red carpet fashion we're always most excited about. 

    From Holly Willoughby in a gorgeous fairytale frock to Vogue Williams' daring De La Vali design and Rochelle Humes' burgundy bodycon dress, keep scrolling to discover the most glamorous red carpet looks from the 2023 NTAs in our best dressed gallery...

    121

    Holly Willoughby

    Holly Willoughby had a princess worthy moment as she led the red carpet at the NTAs. Channelling old school glamour, the This Morning host put on a brave face amid ongoing Phillip Schofield scrutiny, ensuring her outfit took centre stage. Holly looked beautiful in a purple Suzanne Neville dress with a strapless design and Mappin & Webb jewellery.

    221

    Rochelle Humes

    Rochelle Humes looked gorgeous in a figure-hugging red dress with a high neck and a floor-skimming fit. The ITV star matched the hue of her bodycon attire with a slick of scarlet lipstick and wore her hair in soft waves.

    321

    Michelle Keegan and Mark Wright

    Fresh from completing their dream home, Mark Wright and Michelle Keegan put on a loved-up display on the NTAs red carpet. Michelle wowed in a sparkly black co-ord comprised of fitted trousers and a cropped blazer for her rare outing with husband Mark.

    421

    AJ Odudu

    AJ Odudu looked amazing in a floor-sweeping white dress in a halterneck style. Thehost turned heads in her backless, Grecian inspired number as she sashayed down the red carpet.

    521

    Helen Flanagan

    Former actress Helen Flanagan put on a demure display in a strapless design by Karen Millen. The soft khaki dress fitted her like a glove and was accessorized with gold jewellery.

    621

    Emma Willis

    Emma Willis looked impossibly chic in a white suit for her big red carpet moment. The presenter exuded cool girl vibes in her high-waisted trousers and loose jacket, worn over a black bralette.

    721

    Vicky McClure

    Newlywed Vicky McClure was the picture of elegance in a black dress with a cowl neckline and quirky ruched detailing. The Line of Duty actress completed her eveningwear with a pair of patent black stilettos. 

    821

    Alex Scott

    How cool did Alex Scott look in her white caped dress? The column gown was timeless, with chunky gold earrings giving the outfit a modern twist.

    921

    Amy Dowden

    pro Amy Dowden looked like she'd stepped straight out of a fairytale in her beautiful tulle dress with a Bardot neckline. Putting on a brave face amid her ongoing cancer battle, Amy looked absolutely radiant in her blush pink gown.

    1021

    Vogue Williams

    Vogue Williams took a major style risk - and it definitely paid off. The podcast host looked drop dead gorgeous in a De La Vali sequin dress with a halterneck cut and hood detailing.

    1121

    Alison Hammond

    favourite Alison Hammond rocked a plunging black dress with pretty sequin detailing. The gown boasted cape detailing and was perfectly offset by her sleek hairdo. She always pulls it out of the bag, doesn't she! 

    1221

    Alex Jones

    host Alex Jones looked amazing in a white A-line mini dress with feathered cuffs. Upping the glamour, Alex donned a pair of silver heels and hoop earrings.

    1321

    Tasha Ghouri

    star Tasha Ghouri looked sensational in a purple feathered dress as she sashayed down the red carpet. The reality star completed her attire with a matching clutch bag and wore her long blonde longs down in loose waves.

    1421

    Leomie Anderson

    How stunning did Leomie Anderson look? The supermodel was a vision in a strapless black dress with a voluminous train. Striking her best model pose, the gorgeous star stole the show when she presented an award on the night.

    1521

    Maura Higgins

    Maura Higgins stole the show in a daring cut-out dress by Khanum’s with pearl draping. The star looked gorgeous in the custom-designed number which showcased her toned abs.

    1621

    Mollie King

    The Saturdays singer Mollie King did her best Sienna Miller impression in a silky slip dress. The two-tone number boasted a lace trim and a delicate white silk skirt - perfection!

    1721

    Olivia Attwood

    Making a statement in satin, Olivia Attwood looked incredible in a green dress with a thigh-split cut. She styled her long blonde locks in a sleek style and she accessorized with jewel-tipped shoes.

    1821

    Ashley James

    Ashley James was radiant in a beautiful pink dress with oversized sleeve detailing. The look was complete thanks to a pair of peep-toe heels and a silver sparkling clutch. 

    1921

    Susanna Reid

    Susanna Reid was a vision in a Raishma dress, chosen by ITV stylist to the stars Debbie Harper. Looking ravishing in the one-shoulder, sequinned number, the GMB presenter showcased her new choppy bob to perfection.

    2021

    Kate Garraway

    Kate Garraway looked super chic in a white suit from Karen Millen. The host styled her honey blonde bob in a bouncy blow dry, giving her ensemble an 80s vibe.

    2121

    Ant and Dec

    We couldn't help but give a shout-out to Ant and Dec! The dynamic duo flashed beaming grins as they waited to see whether they had picked up the Best Presenter award for the 22nd consecutive year.
