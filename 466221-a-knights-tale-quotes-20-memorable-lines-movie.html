A Knight's Tale quotes: 20 memorable lines from the movie

February 2024 · 7 minute read

A Knight’s Tale opened in theatres on May 11, 2001, with much fanfare. The film featured young stars like Heath Ledger in the lead and received great appreciation and criticism. Twenty years later, the film's influence can still be felt. One of its most interesting things is its impressive collection of quotable lines. So, what are the most outstanding A Knight’s Tale quotes?

Today, there are big blockbusters with a big audience and unappreciated but still good low-budget films. Then some movies are ahead of their time and get appreciated as they age. For example, A Knight's Tale has surpassed critics’ expectations to stay relevant for over 20 years.

So, what is it about the movie that makes it captivating? Of course, the theme is a big deal, and you cannot miss meaningful words occasionally in the characters' speech. So, which quotes from Knight's Tale are the best?
