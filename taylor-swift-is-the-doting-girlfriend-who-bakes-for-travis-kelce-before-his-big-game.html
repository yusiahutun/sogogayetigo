Taylor Swift is the doting girlfriend who bakes for Travis Kelce before his big game

Taylor Swift is sprinkling extra sweetness into her love life and proving to be the best ‘role model’ for young kids.

It is not a secret anymore that pop music queen Taylor Swift is lovestruck with Travis Kelce. In recent interviews, the celebrity couple opened up about their honest feelings for each other. Fans are ready to believe that Travis Kelce is The 1 for the Grammy-winning celebrity.

Taylor Swift’s sweetest gesture for Travis Kelce

She sells out stadiums worldwide, sings to thousands in the audience, and bakes for her Lover as a pre-game ritual. She is the icon of youth: Taylor Alison Swift. Former NFL player Bernie Kosar, who recently attended a pre-game soiree hosted by Travis, shared an incident that shows Taylor is gaining the title of the most doting girlfriend of the year.

Bernie revealed: She made her homemade cinnamon rolls for Travis for pre-game meal, I’m a juicer right now trying to be vegan and gluten-free, but I absolutely had to sample some of that cinnamon roll on game day.”

Along with baking sweet treats for Trav’s pre-game guests, Bernie also shared the music legend’s genuine attention toward everyone.

“You couldn’t have a better role model for young parents, young kids out there to look up to her. Her genuine attention to people and paying attention and consideration — oh, it was just spectacular to watch,” he told AM 560 Sports WQAM.

Travis is mesmerized by Taylor’s presence

Travis Kelce is not going to stop talking about Tay’s mesmerizing presence and how even the fans of the opposition team go all out cheering for her, showing the magnitude of her impact.

You don’t see an entire home team fan base go insane for somebody wearing the opposite team’s colors. Just shows you how amazing that girl is. They went absolutely insane when they showed Taylor on the screen, yes.”

It looks like the admiration is mutual for the It couple of the year and, with the holiday season coming, sources claim that Taylor is ready to make new memories with her NFL heartthrob beau at their new Kansas City home.

To show Taylor how serious Trav is about the relationship, he has apparently prepared surprise gifts and the Epiphany crooner will be spoiled this Christmas.
