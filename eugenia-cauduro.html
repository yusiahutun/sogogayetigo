About

Eugenia Cauduro is a 55 year old Mexican Actress. Born Eugenia Cauduro Rodríguez on 20th December, 1968 in Mexico City, Distrito Federal, Mexico, she is famous for her roles as Magdalena Arredón in the Televisa telenovela Alguna vez tendremos alas, Julia Moreno in the Televisa telenovela Niña Amada Mia, Araceli Montalbán in the Televisa telenovela El Amor No Tiene Precio, Vanessa Coronel de Alcázar in the Televisa telenovela Teresa, Dolores "Lolita" Martínez de Chinrios in the Televisa telenovela Abismo de pasion, Leonora Fuenmayor in the Golden Premier/UniMás/Telehit HD/Canal 5 teen drama series Gossip Girl Acapulco, and Magdalena Murillo in the Televisa telenovela El color de la pasión.
