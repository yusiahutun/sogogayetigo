Meaning of Been Ballin by Lil Uzi Vert

March 2024 Â· 3 minute read

In Lil Uzi Vert's catchy and energetic track, "Been Ballin," the overall theme centers around the exhilarating lifestyle of fame and wealth. The song exudes confidence and boasts about the artist's lavish and extravagant lifestyle. However, beneath the surface, there are deeper, unexpected themes that emerge from various standout lyrics in the song.One notable lyric that stands out is, "Everyday I'm shot calling, Imma spray champagne, make it rain." This line not only emphasizes Uzi's extravagant spending habits but also hints at the power he holds within the industry. Here, the theme of control and influence emerges, suggesting that Uzi is not just a participant in the game but rather a decision-maker in his own right. This theme aligns with the overall theme of the song, emphasizing the artist's elevated status and his ability to dictate his own success.Another interesting lyric is, "You know that I'm rocking Phillip Lim, yeah." While seemingly just a reference to a fashion brand, this lyric can be interpreted more creatively. By mentioning a specific designer, Uzi is symbolizing his transcendence beyond the realm of ordinary luxury. The reference to high-end fashion represents Uzi's mastery over the materialistic aspects of success, turning his existence into an art form. This theme delves into the idea of creating one's own identity through luxury and personal style.Furthermore, the line, "Money won't stop it, go straight to the top, believe it or not just like Guinness, yeah," adds a surprising theme to the song. While it initially refers to Uzi's unstoppable rise to the pinnacle of fame, the mention of Guinness opens up a new interpretation. Guinness is famously known for its world records, presenting the idea that Uzi is pushing boundaries and aiming to exceed expectations. This theme touches on Uzi's desire to break records, surpass limitations, and become a trailblazer in his field.Lastly, the line, "And if you wanna race, then we gonna riot," provides an interesting avenue for exploring the concept of competition and rivalry. It suggests that Uzi's success can inspire others to strive for greatness, but also warns of the chaos that may arise from heated rivalries. This theme highlights the competitive nature of the industry and the determination Uzi possesses to maintain his position at the top.In conclusion, while "Been Ballin" may superficially appear as an anthem about fame and wealth, delving into the standout lyrics reveals unexpected and imaginative underlying themes. These themes include control and influence, creating one's own identity through luxury, breaking records, and the competitive nature of success. With its catchy hooks and electrifying beats, this song is not only entertaining but also offers a deeper layer of meaning for listeners to explore.
