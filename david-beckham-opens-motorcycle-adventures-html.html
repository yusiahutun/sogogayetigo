David Beckham opens up about his motorcycle adventures

September 2024 Â· 4 minute read

He's long been known for his love of motorbike adventures, having previously travelled around Brazil and its Amazonian jungles riding classic Triumph bikes.

On Wednesday, the 47-year-old former footballer opened up about his decision to take time away and travel, explaining that he 'loves the feeling of freedom' and spending time with his friends in new surroundings.

The husband of Victoria Beckham posted a video to his Instagram account - that also served to promote his own line of fragrances - where he was interviewed while taking a break from his most recent adventure.

Going solo: David Beckham opens up about his motorcycle adventures saying he 'loves the feeling of freedom' of riding off with his friends, in an Instagram post on Wednesday 

Asked why he loves riding so much, Beckham replies: 'Freedom. Clear your head, it's all you have to think about. 

'You're out in the open. Especially when you're in the US and you're riding through the canyons. Different temperatures you have different smells. Being with friends, you know, even when you break down, you don't care. 

He continued: 'Our last ride, we broke down and we were literally in a trailer park for seven hours. And the one guy that was there, had the part that we needed, or he could get the part in a few hours.'
