The 10 Best UGG Tazz Platform Lookalikes to Shop On Amazon &amp; Target

August 2024 · 4 minute read

Ugh, it happened: you waited too long to buy those UGG Tazz platform slippers and now, you can’t find them in stock anywhere. Or, maybe they do have your size available, but not in the colorway you want, and you’re unsure if you’re willing to splurge on a shoe that you only half-love. Not worries. Amazon is selling tons of UGG Tazz alternatives that look super similar to the real thing, and the even better news is that they cost a fraction of the price, all ringing up under $70.

In an effort to make your fall and winter wardrobe complete, we’ve browsed the site and found the best of the best lookalikes. Were you hoping to score some light mustard seed slippers to rock with your leg warmers? Got ’em. Are you feeling the fluffy shearling look but are trying to stick to a budget? No worries — a similar pair is priced at just $39.99.

Check out the UGG Tazz lookalikes available on Amazon (and Target), ahead.

  • Bearpaw Women's Martis Slipper

    Bearpaw is known for making quality slippers, so it’s no surprised they nailed this slip-on style. This no-fuss, easy-to-wear pair is the perfect pick for running errands, and should you get too warm, the wool footbed is removable.

  • Stars Above Amira Suede Clog Slippers 

    $213030% Off

    This lookalike from Target is incredibly spot on—and it’s on sale for just $21 at the retailer in a range of shades, including the classic chestnut color, red, black and a metallic gold. It’s recommended you size up if you’re in-between sizes.

  • CUSHIONAIRE Cozy Mule Platform

    If there’s one lookalike that lives up to the hype, it’s this. Aside from making the rounds on TikTok, the Amazon reviews are pretty convincing, with customers calling them a comfortable, quality pair.

    “Same look as UGG, better price and super comfortable!!” writes one reviewer. “I’ve had for a few months now and they’re holding up great! I always tell people to get them.”

  • Athlefit Women's Fuzzy Platform Slippers

    Slightly different than the original, these platform slippers feature a fuzzy trim. They’re still great for wearing outside, but that additional detail makes them so cozy, you might be tempted to slip them on indoors, too.

  • INPAKSA Women's Platform Slippers

    If you tend to gravitate toward darker colors during the cooler months, you’ll probably want to add this UGG Tazz alternative to your cart. The chestnut colorway will complement you go-to jeans and leggings outfits, while the fleece-lined inside will keep your toes nice and toasty.

  • VARECOLA Faux Fur Platform Slippers

    If you’re on a mission to buy a pair of UGG Tazz slippers in mustard seed, we’ve got good and bad news. The bad news is that they’re impossible to find — stores can hardly keep them in stock, and this particular colorway is a hot commodity. The good news, however, is this pair is almost identical — and it costs just $34.99.

  • Metricfalcon Women's Platform Mini Boots

    Reviewers do recommend ordering a half-size up, but other than that, these slippers have a happy fanbase. They come in three different colors — including black — and customers say they’re eerily similar to the real deal (and just as cozy).

  • Meik Mangni Women's Ultra Mini Platform Fashion Boot

    Technically, this one is less of a slip-on Tazz lookalike and more of short boot style, but we’re including it anyway. One major perk of shopping this pick is that you’ll get some support for the back of your foot, which is a common complaint when it comes to the original mule. Another big draw is the unique — and dreamy! — grayish green color, which is bound to earn you tons of compliments whenever you wear them out.

  • LANSGELING Platform Slippers Mini Boots for Women

    Another pick in a lighter colorway, this pair is described by customers as “cozy, warm, and easy” to take on and off — all great qualities for a winter shoe.

    “These shoes are so comfortable and look exactly like the originals, seriously can barely tell a difference,” writes one happy customer, who also made sure to included a helpful note about sizing. “They are a tiny bit narrow so just make sure to size up!!”

  • NATRAKI Women's Platform Slippers Mini Boots

    While the UGG Tazz Shearling Platform is still shoppable on a handful of sites, it’s also a bit more expensive with a $140 price tag. This alternative is still just as fluffy and fun, but at $39.99, they might be a little easier on your bank account.
