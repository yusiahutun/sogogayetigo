Summer 2023 Bag Trends: Pink on It

Stephane Cardinale - Corbis//Getty Images

A sun-kissed summer outfit includes a blush toned bag. "Pink is a trend across all categories this season," says Nordstrom's De Sole. "After all Pantone’s color of the year is ‘Viva Magenta’. A color that oozes joy and optimism, you’ll find all spectrums of pink within handbags this season, from Valentino and Staud’s hot pinks to Coach and Mugler’s candy hues." And in the most opulent takes, Chanel has updated its classic flap bags in dreamy pink leather.

Summer 2023 Bag Trends: Ludicrously Capacious Bags

WWD//Getty Images

According to the season 4 premiere of Succession, you wouldn't want to be caught lugging an oversized tote—especially not around the upper crust of the Upper East Side. But Landyn Tedrick, site merchandising specialist at Fashionphile, says oversized totes are replacing ultra-mini handbags for summer 2023. It's a matter of practicality, the accessories expert explains: "Summer pool parties, beach days, and weekend getaways pair perfectly with the practicality of a spacious tote. You can’t beat a trend offering convenience and functionality." Make a water-resistant investment in the Goyard Goyardine Saint Louis for vacation or the celebrity-favorite Saint Laurent Icare for slouching around Manhattan.

Summer 2023 Bag Trends: Shh...It's Luxury

Jeremy Moeller//Getty Images

Ostentatious logos and flashy house prints are taking a backseat this summer. Many of summer 2023's most covetable bags belong to the "quiet luxury" movement, defined by well-crafted pieces with no obvious ties to a particular brand. It's a "trend" that isn't actually a trend, because the bags have refined leather and sleek hardware accents that will last decades. But there is a new guard of designers joining the category alongside the polished classics at Hermes and Celine, such as Savette, Objets Daso, and Neous.
