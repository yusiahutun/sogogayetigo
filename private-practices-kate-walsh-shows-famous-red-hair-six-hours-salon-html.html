She's known for her gorgeous, vibrant red hair - and now we know how Private Practice star Kate Walsh keeps her tresses looking so perfect.

Kate spent over six hours getting her hair cut and coloured at the Andy Lecompte Salon in Beverly Hills.

This is the same high-end salon where Lindsay Lohan spent seven hours having her hair dyed from black to blonde back in May.

Kate Walsh clutches her giant water bottle as she leaves the Andy Lecompte salon in Beverly Hills after six hours yesterday

Kate as Dr Addison Montgomery on Private Practice

Kate's red hair has become a trademark for the actress - so she didn't hesitate to reassure everyone that she wasn't planning on any drastic changes.  

She used social networking site Twitter to let her thousands of loyal fans know she was not looking forward to being stuck in a salon for most of the day.

'It’d be so much easier to just unscrew my head and send it over to the salon,' she Tweeted.

When asked if she was going to change her signature red hair, the 42-year-old assured her fans she was merely maintaining her current look.

'Still red, just w/highlights, good for ye olde television program.'

The television programme Kate refers to is U.S. medical TV drama Private Practice, where she stars as neonatal surgeon Addison Montgomery.

Kate has garnered a hugely loyal fanbase ever since she was cast in the second season of Grey's Anatomy back in 2005, of which Private Practice is a spin-off.

She will return to television screens across the U.S. when the third season of Private Practice begins this September.

Kate's divorce from movie executive Alex Young was finalised in February, after just 15 months of marriage.
