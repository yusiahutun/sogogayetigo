Taylor Swift wears Reem Acra for friend's wedding

February 2024 · 1 minute read

Instagram

Taylor Swift is well-known for her famous "girl squad" – but that doesn't mean she isn't still friends with the same people she's known her whole life. 

The singer proved as much this weekend when she played maid of honour at childhood friend Brit Maack's wedding, wearing a gorgeous blush-pink Reem Acra gown. 

"I met her when I was 10 days old, and him in kindergarten," she wrote on Instagram. "Now they're married and I'm the happiest maid of honour ever. Congratulations @britmaack and Ben!"
