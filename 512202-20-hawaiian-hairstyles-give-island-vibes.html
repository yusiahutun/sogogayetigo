20+ Hawaiian hairstyles that will give you island vibes

When you consider changing your hairstyle, a few come to mind. These are styles you have tried before. But without venturing out, you wouldn't have known about the marvellous Hawaiian look. From beachy waves to intricate braids and updos, Hawaiian hairstyles will elevate your style and revamp your look like never before. And it is all about choosing the best.

Nothing is as extraordinary as completing your Hawaiian look with a matching hairstyle that gives you the perfect island vibes. And this doesn't mean just adding flowers and flower crowns. It is going all the way and giving yourself that perfectionist touch that will emanate beach vibes from miles away. So, what are the best easy Hawaiian hairstyles?

List of the best Hawaiian hairstyles to try

Whether heading for a vacation or embracing your tropical side, Hawaiian hairstyles are the way to go. Try it if you haven’t before. But if you want to look like a diva for your upcoming beach holiday, you better ensure you get it right; some examples might be helpful.

1. Short Hawaiian look with a flower

What is a Hawaiian hairstyle without a flower? This short look is something to look forward to as it looks stunning yet reserved. To achieve it, you need to texture your hair with a little wave so it blows in the breeze. Add a little flower to accentuate it, tuck it behind one ear or pull the hair into a braid crown.

2. Long Hawaiian hairstyle

People with long hair have numerous options regarding the Hawaiian look, which is why the long Hawaiian hairstyle is fabulous. You can try many styles with long hair, but this simple one is worth trying as it doesn't require much.

3. Hawaiian curly hairstyle

Curls always give that tropical vibe and are one of the best Hawaiian looks; therefore, if your hair has natural curls, you have the advantage here. You can create a wavy texture if your hair doesn't have a natural curl. On top of that, you can use any Hawaiian hair accessories and products to create the ideal undone curls.

4. Hawaiian style with flowers crown

The flower crown is the most custom addition to any Hawaiian hairstyle. And this look, even without going deep into it, already gives the beach vibe. You can add one or two blossoms for any occasion, but flower crowns are the best choice. Make sure you settle on the right flowers with thick bold hues or soft whites.

6. Messy bun updo

The good thing when you are interested in a tropical look is that you can go for something messy that will fit the occasion. 7. Side fishtail braid

Nothing is as excellent while going to the beach as wearing a style miming aquatic life, and the side fishtail look does precisely that. The fishtail looks charming, and you can wear it as an everyday style. Only the fishtail is styled and has a flower, while the rest of the hair flows gracefully with the perfect waves. 8. Bun with a flower crown

The best way to style your hair is to go for the bun with a flower crown. It is something easy to do. Just brush your hair to achieve the desired softness and then style it into a bun and add the flower crown.

9. Rose crown

By rocking the rose flower crown style, you must try the classic Hawaiian look. And the best flowers are tropical flowers like plumeria and orchids. However, if you cannot access these, you can make a beautiful crown from small colourful flowers within your reach.

10. Rope twisted style

The rope-twisted look is something intricate that you should occasionally try, as it goes well with long hair. And how do you achieve the look? It is easy. You must combine two braids, the rope braid and the traditional three-strand twist braid.

You must start by splitting into two sections and then create the rope by twisting the two sections together.

11. Micro cornrow braids

Braids would be the last thing on anyone’s list when considering Hawaiian hairstyles, but it works amazingly well. The stunning look has solid tropical vibes, but you must blend it with the right colours to produce the desired effect.

You can dress this intricate style up or down. And if you are interested in adding some edge to your appearance, you can pair the braids with a shaved sides hairstyle.

12. Gorgeous fluff hairstyle

The gorgeous fluff afro hairstyle is perfect if you are looking for something out-of-the-ordinary. There is no doubt that the magnificent hairstyle is ideal for any Hawaiian occasion. Another great thing about the hairstyle is that it is easy to manage and can be styled however desired.

13. Floral headband

Another way to achieve the perfect Hawaiian look is to top up your hairstyle with a floral headband. One thing about the headband is that it adds an exquisite touch of elegance to a casual style. On top of this, you can wear the floral headband with various outfits mixed with unique shapes and colours.

14. Hawaiian bun

The crotchet puff curl medium hair is classy, combining the free-spirited vibe of a Hawaiian with a modern, stylish twist. You can achieve this look by using crotchet hair extensions.

What is the Hawaiian culture of hair?

In the Hawaiian culture, long hair is considered feminine, so it is common to spot ladies with hair across their waist. Men also preserve their hair, and there are so many male Hawaiian hairstyles.

People in Hawaii use natural ingredients from the islands, and food plays a massive role in creating such products. But of course, when it comes to usage and knowing what product works for you, you must know your Hawaiian hair type.
