If you’re a fan of watching celeb skincare tutorials or reading about what stars keep in their makeup bags, you probably are well aware of A-listers’ go-to La Mer moisturizer—Kim Kardashian West and Chrissy Teigen are just a few of the many famous fans. While it’s tempting to want to splurge on luxury skincare and get a complexion like Chrissy’s, your wallet will most likely not be happy with you after dropping $380 on it.

It’s tricky because you want what’s best for your skin, but you also want to save money. However, in the wise words of Hannah Montana, you can get the best of both worlds thanks to more affordable alternatives that work just as well, according to reviewers.

So, what exactly makes this moisturizer so coveted and celeb-approved? The main ingredients in Crème De La Mer include seaweed extract, a concoction the brand calls “Miracle Broth,” and lime tea. It also has glycerin, panthenol, petrolatum, and lanolin, as well. This rich formula heals, protects, moisturizes, and most importantly, reduces the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. 

Now that you know what the iconic Crème De La Mer is made of, you can understand why these five alternatives totally fit the bill (hint: they pack a similar laundry list of soothing ingredients into each formula). Best of all, none of these alternatives cost more than $64.

In fact, three of them are discounted during Amazon’s Prime Early Access Sale, which ends later tonight, so get on these deals ASAP! All you have to do to snag these amazing markdowns is sign up for a Prime membership or free 30-day trial.

This face cream combines seaweed extract, collagen, elastin, and sodium hyaluronate to nourish, smooth, and hydrate your skin. “La Mer is lovely and a nice splurge, but this stuff is really comparable, and a fraction of the cost. I will be a regular customer,” wrote one shopper. Other reviewers say that the thick formula helps them achieve “baby-soft skin” without having to suffer through any breakouts.

This night cream boasts a ton of skin-benefitting ingredients like ceramides, peptides, hyaluronic acid, and niacinamide. These elements work together to keep your natural skin barrier healthy, combat visible signs of aging, reduce redness, calm, and hydrate.
