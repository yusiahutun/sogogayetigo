Meet James Quentin, Monica Adams Husband

Monica Adams is married to her husband James Quentin. They reportedly married in early October 2015 in Augusta, MO. The happy couple was surrounded by family, friends, and several FOX 2 co-workers.

In October 2019, Monica shared on her FB, “4 years ago on this night it was one of the most magical and I was so giddy to pull off this long-standing memory of surprising my baby on our rehearsal night with his Harley for his wedding gift! I love you James with all of my heart and can’t wait to spend some time away with you for our anniversary! Always and Forever I will love and honor you and protect your heart!”

A few days after on their anniversary day in the same year, Monica again posted, “4 years ago, I was shivering with goosebumps as I rode the carriage to you and knew for the rest of my life I would be in the arms of the man who I would love for life!! You are truly everything I never knew I needed and everything that made me feel complete l. I love you my James and I will honor you for eternity. Thank you for being my forever protector!.”

Monica also gushed about her husband’s surprise for her 50th birthday. She wrote, “A truly incredible 50th birthday celebration. Jim surprised me with a weekend getaway in Hermann and we couldn’t ask for prettier weather. Our cabin at the Inn at Hermannhoff reminded me of a Tuscan cottage. Amazing dinner at Alpenhorn and then a weekend visiting distilleries and wineries. The Wurst haus smelled amazing and Jim loved the cigar bar…then on our way home checking on my friend’s new wine bar Oak and Front in Washington MO. Wow so proud of her.. it is beautiful.”

She concluded the post, “Started my birthday with my brother Craig and his fiancé Amy at Sqwires with a wonderful breakfast and ended hanging with my mom who watched our fur babies and made me a money tree… she is so amazing..my brother Brian and sister in law Tina came by last night to celebrate. Love you all. My trooper of a hubby just had knee surgery and still spoiled me. He’s the best.”

James Quentin And Monica Adams Children

The couple doesn’t share any children and Monica has spoken about it on her Facebook. In one post, she wrote, “I know you sent Tessa and now Adelaide to be my fur children since Jim and I were robbed of that chance.”

However, Monica has previously shared that James has a son named Brad. On Father’s Day 2021, Monica took to her FB and wrote, “Happy Father’s Day to the love of my life! I am so happy he was able to spend an amazing vacation in Cabo with his son Brad! We had such an amazing time and love you so much! #fathersday #fatherson.“

James Quentin Age

In August 1963, James Quentin was born. He turned 59 years old in August 2022.

James Quentin Job

According to Open Payrolls, James Quentin worked as a lieutenant for the Division of Patrol in 2017 and St. Louis County, MO in 2018. During that period he received a yearly salary of $90,480 and $45.33/HR, according to the website.

Monica has also supported her husband’s career and many people who worked in the fire departments, police, and military. She wrote, “We must believe in a safe nation, otherwise those cowards win! You will NOT make me look over my shoulder. I trust in the work our brothers and sisters in Police, Fire and the military do for us to keep us safe at home with our families. I am honored to have one of the strongest officers I know by my side every day.”

She then praised her husband writing, “I love you James Quentin, and am so proud that you chose the profession you did as a child…one of the most selfless professions ever! Thank you to all for protecting us some 17 years after the worst attack on U.S. soil. I will NEVER forget.”

James Quentin Family

There isn’t much about James Quentin’s family. Some of the people he is related to are Cassidy Hutchings, James Cooksey, Amanda Hutchings, and Kristie Ingrams. His wife was born to Miles and Jacqueline Adams. Miles died at the age of 59. Her mom is currently 84 years old.

In January 2023, Brian Adams will be 58 years old and Monica’s other brother Craig will be 55. Brian attended St. Thomas Aquinas Catholic High School before enrolling at the University of Missouri-St. Louis to study biology.
