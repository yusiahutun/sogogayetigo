10 of the best black female bodybuilders

When you think about muscular physiques and bodybuilding, who comes to mind? Probably the biggest men bodybuilders. Interestingly, building muscles has been, for some time now, an integral part of women's sports.

You can easily find numerous African American female bodybuilders on Instagram with physiques that even some male gym enthusiasts dream about. Who are these female strong-women that are making the bodybuilding industry shine?

1. Roxie Beckles

Roxie is one of the best black female bodybuilders with an amazing structure. She also works as an online and contests preparation coach. Her ascent in the female bodybuilding industry was in 2013 when she won the Team Universe National Championship.

Roxie has contributed massively in the fitness industry, working extensively with the New York Road Runners Foundation as a running coach for their kid’s programmes. Additionally, Roxie’s extensive career in bodybuilding has allowed her to create effective weight loss, core strengthening and flexibility programmes.

7. Ernestine Shepherd

Ernestine Shepherd is famous for being the oldest female competitive bodybuilder in the world. She had even been included in the Guinness World Records in 2010. Ernestine was born in 1936, making her 74 years old in 2020.

She obtained bodybuilding tiles at the age of 56 and even participated in nine marathons. Ernestine leads exercise classes for seniors at the Union Memorial United Methodist Church in Baltimore. Indeed, she is an inspiration to numerous aspiring bodybuilders and an influential personality you ought to follow on Instagram.
