21 Best Places to Order Thanksgiving Dinners To Go 2023

July 2024 · 6 minute read
1

Cracker Barrel

Cracker Barrel

Cracker Barrel restaurants will be open for dine-in service on Thanksgiving Day, but if you'd rather stay home, they offer complete Heat n' Serve options, including a Thanksgiving Heat n’ Serve Turkey Feast and Thanksgiving Heat n’ Serve Turkey Family Dinner. Don't miss out on the new Country Fried Turkey catering option. Preorder now and pick it up at your local store. Add a cinnamon roll pie to serve for breakfast.

2

Metro Diner

Metro Diner

Metro Diner's heat-and-serve meal includes a choice of ham or turkey, mashed potatoes, gravy, green beans, stuffing, cranberry sauce, Hawaiian dinner rolls, and cornbread stuffing or mac and cheese. They offer meals for 1, 4, or 8. Check their location guide for a restaurant near you. Restaurants are open for table service and pick-ups on November 23 until 2:30 p.m.

3

Golden Corral

Courtesy of Golden Corral

Personally, we love the idea of loading up the family and hitting the Golden Corral buffet on Thanksgiving. But for an at-home celebration, check out their holiday feast to go that serves 6-8. You can also get individual holiday meals to go.

4

Bob Evans

Bob Evans

The Thanksgiving in a Box meal from Bob Evans offers the choice of three entrée options (turkey, pot roast, or ham), plus sides and pies, serving four, eight or up to 10 people and starting at $79.99.

5

Denny's

Mike Mozart

For an almost-effortless choice, pick up a turkey and dressing dinner pack from Denny's. The meal includes carved turkey breast, stuffing, gravy, cranberry sauce, bread, and a choice of broccoli or corn. Order online at least 24 hours in advance.

6

Ponderosa and Bonanza Steakhouses

Ponderosa and Bonanza Steakhouses

If you don't feel like dining in at the buffet, take a platter to-go. They include ham or turkey, mashed potatoes, stuffing, cranberry sauce, veggies, and a roll. Prices start at $12. Check the store locator to find the Ponderosa or Bonanza location near you.

7

Whole Foods

7

Whole Foods

Credit: Whole Foods

Of course, you can pick up all of the ingredients for a from-scratch meal from Whole Foods, or you can order the complete ready-to-heat feast. They have several options, including this roast turkey dinner for 8 (turkey, green beans, mashed potatoes, stuffing, gravy, cranberry-orange sauce), a smaller meal for 4, a feast for 12, and even a vegan meal.

8

The Capital Grille

The Capital Grille

Take care of the turkey yourself and then pick up all the sides from The Capital Grille on November 22 or 23 and heat them up at home. Their delectable menu includes brioche stuffing, gravy, mashed potatoes, French green beans, cranberry-pear chutney, and pecan-crusted sweet potatoes with hot honey.

9

The Honey Baked Ham Co.

9

The Honey Baked Ham Co.

Credit: HoneyBaked

Yes, you can pick up a ham from HoneyBaked, but they also have full Thanksgiving meals, including a turkey breast meal for 4 to 6 with a turkey breast, two sides, and rolls. Or, please everyone with the ham and turkey feast. Don't miss out on the new Take & Bake Ham & Cheddar Biscuits.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

10

Bravo! Italian Kitchen

Bravo! Italian Kitchen

Bravo! is offering complete Thanksgiving dinners. Their options serve between 3 and 20 and include turkey, gravy, Italian sausage stuffing, roasted garlic mashed potatoes, green beans, cranberry sauce, and pumpkin pie. Pick it up cold or hot and ready to eat. Preorder before November 22.

11

IHOP

John Greim//Getty Images

Get your holiday started on the right foot with a family feast from IHOP. The Breakfast Feast for four comes with buttermilk pancakes, belgian waffles, or French toast, scrambled eggs, hash browns, and bacon or sausage links. Check the store locator for hours. This year, they have a new Homestyle Turkey & Stuffing Dinner and a Homestyle Turkey & Stuffing Melt.

12

Popeyes

Roberto Machado Noa//Getty Images

Did you know the Louisiana-based fried chicken restaurant offers Cajun-flavored turkeys for Thanksgiving? Find the restaurant nearest you using their store locator, and call ahead to place your order. Prices start at $59.99 for a 13-16 pound turkey. Grab some green beans, mashed potatoes, and biscuits to go with it, and you'll be eating in no time! The restaurant typically offers turkeys shipped to your door, but they are sold out for 2023.

13

Publix

Publix

Many grocery stores offer fully cooked Thanksgiving dinners. This year Publix has a Boar's Head turkey dinner, which serves 3 to 4 ($55.99) and comes with Boar’s Head turkey and family-friendly sides like mashed potatoes, dressing, cranberry-orange relish, and marshmallow delight. Other options include a smoked ham dinner and a fully cooked turkey breast dinner that serves 7 to 10.

14

Cheddar's Scratch Kitchen

Cheddar's Scratch Kitchen

Pick up a family bundle on November 23 and you'll be all set on Thanksgiving Day. Choose from crowd-pleasing entrées like hand-breaded chicken tenders or grilled salmon with two family-sized sides and a half-dozen signature Honey Butter Croissants. Find a location near you to order.

15

Boston Market

Christopher Nastri

You can get your favorite items a la carte at Boston Market, or order a complete, ready-to-heat Thanksgiving meal for 6 or 12 people. The roasted turkey breast meal for 6 includes a roasted turkey breast, spinach-artichoke dip, stuffing, cranberry-walnut relish, mashed potatoes, gravy, rolls, and apple or pumpkin pie.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

16

Brio Italian Grille

Brio

Pick up a hot or cold feast from Brio. Their meal serves 3 to 20 and includes turkey, Italian sausage stuffing, vegetables, mashed potatoes, cranberry sauce, and pumpkin pie. Preorder now from a location near you.

17

Harris Teeter

John Greim

Live near a Harris Teeter? Order your precooked holiday dinner from this grocery chain. Head to the store to place your order, and make sure you do it at least 24 hours in advance.

18

Buca di Beppo

Buca di Beppo

This Italian chain offers a traditional Thanksgiving dinner including turkey, gravy, mashed potatoes, spicy Italian sausage stuffing, green beans, cranberry sauce, bread and butter, and pumpkin pie. Meals serve 3 or 6 and catering options serve up to 20. Preorder now and pick up a complete feast on Thanksgiving Day.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

19

Kroger

Scott Olson//Getty Images

Order your whole precooked Thanksgiving meal from Kroger. They offer a variety of proteins (smoked or roasted whole turkey, turkey breast, prime rib, and ham) paired with green bean casserole, sweet potato soufflé, mashed potatoes, gravy, Hawaiian rolls, dressing, and a pumpkin pie.

20

Harry & David

20

Harry & David

Credit: Harry & David

For the ultimate convenience, order the whole Thanksgiving meal to be delivered right to your door. This gourmet menu from Harry & David includes ready-to-heat turkey, apple-sausage stuffing, black truffle and almond green beans, brown sugar sweet potatoes, pumpkin cheesecake, gravy, and spiced cranberry chutney.
