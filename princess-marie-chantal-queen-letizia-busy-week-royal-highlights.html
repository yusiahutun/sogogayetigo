Princess Marie-Chantal and Queen Letizia's busy week plus other royal highlights

Queen Letizia of Spain jetted to Milan for World Food Day - with the focus on cutting food waste globally - where she met with Jose Graziano da Silva, Director General of FAO (United Nations Organization for Food and Agriculture - of which Letizia is also an ambassador) and Ertharin Cousin, Executive Director of the World Food Programme.Photo: Getty Images Princess Marie-Chantal had a busy week escorting her son Prince Constantine-Alexios around several Ivy League universities, bumping into Tatiana Von Furstenberg and her daughter Antonia and celebrating sister Pia's birthday.Photo: Instagram/@mariechantal22 Lady Kitty Spencer prepped for a posh night out at the Women of the Year Awards at the Bulgari Spa.Photo: Instagram/@kitty.spencer ¡Olé!
