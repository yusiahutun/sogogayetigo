Mumias East MP Peter Salasya was left tongue-tied after viewing a photo of Amber Ray for the first time.

What did Salasya say about Amber Ray?

Salasya checked out the socialite after telling Oga Obinna he did not know who Amber was.

Curious about why Salasya was unaware, Obinna took the MP through a whirlwind of emotion as they bantered over her beauty.
