8 Alternatives to Temu for Affordable Shopping

April 2024 · 6 minute read

Since its launch in late 2022, Temu has quickly grown in popularity and is currently one of the most used shopping sites in the US. This company’s swift rise is all thanks to its massive selection of products that are offered at incredibly low prices.

If you’ve enjoyed shopping on Temu, you’ll want to check out these alternatives that offer extensive product selections and excellent value.

1. Wish

Wish is an online shopping platform that offers various styles of clothing, accessories, gadgets, beauty supplies, and more. Thanks to the plethora of discounts and promotions, Wish helps you save significantly on each purchase.

What’s more, the platform often runs special promotions during the holidays to make shopping more affordable for users. If you decide to shop on Wish, check the reviews and ratings for each product before you zero in on anything.

Highlights:

  • Wish takes anywhere between 15 and 45 days to deliver products.
  • Returns are accepted within 30 days from the date of delivery
  • Products can be priced as low as $1.
  • You get to earn a stamp for each day that you log into your Wish account. You can unlock discounts of up to 50% once you accumulate seven stamps.

2. Romwe

Romwe is an online store based in China that ships globally, including to the United States. The platform offers an extensive collection of apparel, footwear, accessories, and more, on its platform.

Like Temu, the online retailer offers several promotions and discounts the first time you order. The quality of products, however, can vary widely, and, thus, it’s best to check the product reviews before you purchase anything.

Highlights:

  • Romwe takes between eight and 12 business days to deliver orders. This is not including the processing time, which may take up to six days.
  • Express shipping is free for orders over $159.
  • Products you purchase can be returned within 60 days. Note that only your first order can be returned for free–subsequent orders will be charged a return fee of $7.99.

3. Dollar Tree

With over 16,000 stores located across the United States and a growing online presence, Dollar Tree is one of the most popular discount stores in the country.

Dollar Tree is technically no longer a dollar store, given that the company has raised the prices of most goods to $1.25. Still, the deals are hard to beat. From craft supplies to candy to linens, Dollar Tree’s website (and stores) are packed with reasonably-priced products.

Highlights:

  • Dollar Tree does not offer refunds. However, you can exchange any unopened item if you have the original receipt.
  • The retailer offers free in-store pickup.
  • You can also have orders shipped to your address. This will take anywhere between four and five days, after order processing.

4. Five Below

Five Below is one of the best value retailers in the country, offering high-quality tech products, toys, beauty supplies, and more, at affordable prices. Unlike other retailers, Five Below offers many trendy products that cater to pre-teens and teenagers.

The company has over 1,200 stores across the country and an easy-to-navigate website, which is worth checking out if you’re looking for fun, well-priced products.

Highlights:

  • Most products listed on Five Below are priced between $1 and $5.
  • A flat-rate shipping fee of $7.95 is applicable on all orders.
  • Orders typically arrive in five to seven business days.
  • Returns are allowed, but only at store locations.

5. AliExpress

AliExpress is an online marketplace that is made up of independent sellers based out of China. The platform has served as a go-to destination for budget shoppers looking to score products across categories, from fashion to kitchen appliances to gadgets, and more.

Like Temu, AliExpress offers some of the most affordable products online. In fact, several items on the platform cost under $1.

Highlights:

  • Standard Shipping through AliExpress takes between 15 and 45 days.
  • If you opt for AliExpress Premium Shipping, your delivery will be expedited. In this case, you can expect to receive it within 15 days.
  • Returns are typically free, but you will have to open a dispute with AliExpress within 15 days of receiving the item.
  • Customer service is easy to access and is often swift.

6. Zulily

If you’re looking to score deals on well-known brands like Levi’s, Champion, and Calvin Klein, you’ll definitely want to check out Zulily. The retailer offers all types of products on its website, including clothing, accessories, home decor, electronics, and more.

Zulily’s shipping works a little differently. If you place an order, Zulily typically waits for a few days to ship one bulk order. Due to this distribution strategy, you won’t receive your merchandise immediately. It typically takes at least eight days to receive Zulily orders.

Highlights:

  • You can snag savings of up to 70% when you shop at Zulily.
  • Once you place your first order, you can place additional orders for free for the rest of the day.
  • If you purchased a return-eligible item, you can return the product within 30 days.

7. Walmart

This big-box retail giant sells pretty much everything you could ever need, from electronics to clothing to home goods, and more, all under one roof. Whether you shop on Walmart’s website or in-store, this means you can save time and effort by purchasing everything you need in one go.

Walmart’s commitment to offering affordably priced goods is unmatched. The retailer’s frequent promotions and everyday low prices allow shoppers to snag great deals.

Highlights:

  • Most items can be returned for free within 90 days of the date of delivery, either to a store or by mail.
  • Frequent shoppers can join the Walmart+ program to get free shipping on all orders and other perks.
  • Walmart offers expedited shipping for select orders.

8. Family Dollar

If you like shopping at dollar stores, you’ve got to give Family Dollar a try. The retailer’s deals help you score big on apparel, beauty supplies, tech devices, and more.

In fact, there are few dollar stores in the US that offer the kind of variety and savings that Family Dollar does. The retailer also lists customer reviews for products sold on its website to help you make better shopping choices.

Highlights:

  • Products purchased online can be returned within 30 days from the date of purchase.
  • You can download the app to clip free Smart Coupons.
  • Family Dollar offers same-day delivery via Instacart.

Staying Safe While Shopping for Bargains Online

While shopping online can undoubtedly be convenient, there are also risks associated with making digital transactions. Given this, it’s important to take steps, like verifying the website’s URL and checking customer reviews, to keep yourself safe while shopping online.
