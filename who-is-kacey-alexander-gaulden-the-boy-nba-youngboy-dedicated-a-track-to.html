Who Is Kacey Alexander Gaulden, the Boy NBA Youngboy Dedicated a Track To?

May 2024 · 4 minute read

Kacey Alexander Gaulden achieved fame even before he was born into this world because of who his parents are. Kacey is the son of famous rapper NBA YoungBoy and was born in February 2019, making the rapper a father for the sixth time.

Kacey’s father got famous after he started uploading his rap songs to his channel on YouTube. He was in jail when the “Win or Lose” music video went viral.  

YoungBoy welcomed Kacey with his former lover Jania Bania. He has several other baby mamas and 11 kids. Here is all you need to know about Kacey, his sixth child. 

Who Is Kacey Alexander Gaulden’s Mother? 

NBA YoungBoy welcomed Kacey with Jania Bania. She is a social media influencer and model popular for posting selfies, fashion, and lifestyle photos on her Instagram account. 

The account, named “janiameshell,” has over 3.5 million followers. She also enjoys sharing content that promotes the fashion brand, Fashion Nova. 

Bania dated Kentrell Gaulden, AKA rapper NBA YoungBoy, but their relationship ended in mid-2018. She gave birth to their son in 2019. Later that year, she started dating Dejounte Murray. She has also been romantically linked to boxer Devin Haney. 

In January 2023, she revealed she was expecting her first child with Dejounte Murray.

Kacey Alexander Gaulden’s Net Worth

Kacey Alexander Gaulden is yet to come of legal age, so he still lives with his parents. He is too young to have amassed any measure of personal wealth. However, his famous father is believed to be worth over $6 million. With his money, Kacey lives a privileged life and is well cared for.

Kacey Alexander Gaulden Brothers 

At the time of this writing, Kacey is believed to have five brothers. His oldest brother is Kayden Gaulden, whom his father welcomed at the young age of 16.

In the years that followed, the rapper got busy, welcoming ten more kids in addition to Kayden. Among them, six are boys. YoungBoy’s youngest was born in 2013, but there is very little detail on the child except that the rapper had him with his ninth baby mama, Drew Valentina. 

News of his child with Valentina came several months after YoungBoy wed his longtime girlfriend, Jazlyn Mychelle, in January 2023. The couple welcomed their second child together in 2022. Valentina has chosen to keep the gender and name of their child a secret, so it is unclear if Kacey got another brother or sister. 

Kacey Alexander Gaulden’s Birthday 

Kacey Alexander Gaulden celebrates his birthday on February 3 every year. His parents are on cordial terms, and they usually make the day special for him. Being there for his kids is important for YoungBoy, who is working hard as a rapper to ensure his kids continue to enjoy the luxurious lifestyle. 

How Old Is Kacey Alexander Gaulden? 

Since Kacey was born on February 3, 2019, he is four years old as of this year, making him one of the rapper’s youngest sons. 

Kacey Alexander Gaulden Parents

Kacey Alexander Gaulden was born to famous rapper NBA YoungBoy and a social media influencer/model named Jania Bania. YoungBoy has made a name for himself, releasing hits back to back in hip-hop. However, he is among the most controversial rappers on the music scene.

In Kacey Alexander Gaulden’s case, he wrote a rap song dedicated as a tribute to him. The song, titled “Kacey Talk,” was released as a single initially but was re-released as part of the EP “Tracks from Top,” referring to the rapper’s upcoming album “Top.” The song was accompanied by a music video starring YoungBoy and two of his kids, including Kacey.

Kacey Alexander Gaulden Age
