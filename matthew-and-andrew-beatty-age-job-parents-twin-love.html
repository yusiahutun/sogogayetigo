Meet Matthew And Andrew Beatty! Age, Job, Parents

June 2024 · 5 minute read

Two Boston twins Matthew and Andrew Beatty are starring in Prime Video’s Twin Love. Who are their parents? What job do they do? When’s their birthday?

If you came here looking for the answers then you’re in the right place. Scroll down and read all about them here.

Matthew And Andrew Beatty On Prime’s Twin Love

Matthew and Andrew Beatty are the stars of Prime’s a unique dating show Twin Love. The show invited 14 identical twins who were on a quest to find love in a social experiment setting.

Their bio read: “From a Military family, Matt and Andrew were born in Germany. They moved around a lot when they were younger, which forced them to break out of their shells. Their big personalities are matched by their signature “sick fits.” These two claim their personalities (and appearances) are so outgoing that they’ve been recognized by strangers in different cities for years.”

“Twin Love is a social dating experiment that explores the love lives of identical twins. Ten sets of twins will be split up and placed into two houses, creating identical casts, where they’ll begin a unique and compelling search for love,” the official synopsis read. “With double the fun and drama, the series will discover if their inherent similarities extend to their romantic desires. When separated from their twin brothers and sisters—some for the first time ever—will they pursue identical partners? Or will their choices be dramatically different?”

The featured twins will have to find out if their similarities translate to their romantic choices or how different they will be because of the show’s unusual style. The hosts of Twin Love, which is based on a model developed by ITV Studios Netherlands, are Brie and Nikki Garcia, better known as the Bella twins from Total Bellas and WWE.

The show premiered on 17 November 2023.

Before the show’s debut Matthew and Andrew announced on their joint IG, “Twin Love featuring your fav ginger twins premiering on Nov. 17th on @primevideo #twins #twinlove.”

Matthew And Andrew Beatty Age

Matthew and Andrew Beatty were born on 8 April 1998. Hence, as of November 2023, they are 25 years old.

Who Are Matthew And Andrew Beatty Parents?

Matthew and Andrew Beatty are the children of John Beatty Sr and Maureen Hart Beatty. The family is likely of Irish descent.

John, who hailed from South Boston, was chosen to be the Chief Marshal of the 2019 St. Patrick’s/Evacuation Day Parade. He was “born down on I Street; raised up on Fourth Street”. John’s mom Carol, still lives in Castle Cove. John was schooled locally, at Saint Augustine’s School and then Don Bosco High. After graduating, he considered enlisting in the Marines but instead was awarded an appointment to West Point, which he accepted.

After completing his four years at West Point, he became an artillery officer assigned to units such as the First Infantry Division in Germany and the Tenth Mountain Division at Fort Drum in Watertown, N.Y.  He was assigned two tours of duty in Iraq (‘05-‘06 and ‘07-‘08). He was a career Army artillery officer, retiring as a Lieutenant Colonel after serving on active duty for 23 years.

John is the Executive Director of the Commonwealth’s Military Asset and Security Strategy Task Force (MASS-TF).

Maureen, his wife, is 60 years old. John himself turned 53 in February 1970.

Besides the two, John and Maureen are also parents to their firstborn John Beatty Jr (@johnnybeattyjr). John played football and lacrosse at Middlebury. He has been working as a Vice President Of Development at Boston Pinnacle Properties since March 2022.

Matthew And Andrew Beatty Job

Moving to the details about Matthew and Andrew Beatty’s job,

Matthew has been working as a Business Development Representative (BDR) at homebldr in Austin, Texas since September 2023. Previously, he worked in sales for Beevo.

Matthew worked as a mover at Olympia Moving & Storage in 2017 to start with. He then served as a Wealth Management Intern at Merrill Lynch in 2018. After that, he was into commercial fishing at F/V Old Speck.

Matthew also worked as a Property Management Intern at Besen Partners and as a Resident Services Coordinator at Kensington Investment Company.

Furthermore, Matthew is a graduate of Fordham Gabelli School of Business and the United States Military Academy at West Point.

Andrew, meanwhile, has been working as a Business Development Representative (BDR) at Adove since August 2023. He worked as an Architecture Recruitment Consultant for Michael Page from December 2022 to July 2023.

Moreover, he also worked as an enterprise BDR at Fairmarkit and CRE Business Development at Hunneman.

Andrew went to the College of the Holy Cross where he studied Political Science and Government.

Matthew and Andrew Beatty both stand tall above 6’1”.
