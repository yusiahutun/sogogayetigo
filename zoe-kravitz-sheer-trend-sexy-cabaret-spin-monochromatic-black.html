Zo Kravitz Enjoyed &quot;Cabaret&quot; in a Sexy All-Black Ensemble

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Zoë Kravitz enjoyed a big night out in New York City on Tuesday, attending Cabaret at the Kit Kat Club at the August Wilson Theatre. And she appeared to channel the electric show with her outfit, but gave it her own twist.

In the Big Little Lies star’s ensemble, sexy cabaret met the sultry sheer trend. Kravitz looked ravishing in an all-black look consisting of an itty-bitty minidress that was completely see-through, except for a narrow band across the bottom hemline and her bust, exposing some serious underboob. Because Cabaret is all about the undergarments, she wore the daring piece (which she cinched at the waist with a small leather belt) over shiny, high-waisted briefs and sheer tights.

Gotham//Getty Images

To help her brave the city breeze, Kravitz threw a long black wool coat over her shoulders, and finished everything off with a small leather purse and glossy, strappy stilettos with a peep-toe design. She kept her jewelry minimal, adding just a pair of dainty drop earrings, slicked her dark locks back into a topknot, and glammed up with bronzy makeup complete with a glossy lip.

The model tends to opt for darker palettes and is a huge champion of the sheer trend, no doubt inspired by the rocker style of her dad, Lenny, long known for his affinity for see-through shirts. In March, she attended Saint Laurent’s lavish pre-Oscars party in the most Zoë Kravitz look. Then, her outfit included a a semi-sheer black gown that featured a wide halterneck that wrapped under each arm to create a completely open back, see-through fabric across the bust and down one leg, and a large piece of opaque fabric that wrapped around her waist to create a floor-length skirt.
