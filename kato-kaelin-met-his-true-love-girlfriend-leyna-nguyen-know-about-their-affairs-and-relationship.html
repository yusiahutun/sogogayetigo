Quick Facts About Kato Kaelin

  • Born on 9 March 1959 in Milwaukee, Wisconsin.
  • He portrayed the character of Bruce Lee in a TV series The Green Hornet and since then, he was widely known as Kato.
  • Completed his graduation from Nicolet High School in Glendale and later studied in the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire.
  • Came in the spotlight for portraying the role of a minor eye witness for the O.J. Simpson murder trial in the movie Beach Fever.
  • Also, starred in a low budget horror movie Night Shadow in the year 1989.
  • Appeared in MADtv, Mr. Show with Bob and David, We Regret to Inform You, The Still Life, National Lampoon's Dorm Daze 2 and Star-ving.
  • Participated in numerous reality and game shows including Celebrity Boot Camp, House Guest, Sunset Tan, Reality Bites Back, The Weakest Link, and Russian Roulette.
  • Worked as a radio host for a short duration on KLSX in Los Angeles in 1995; also worked in National Lampoon and Eye for an Eye.
  • Dedicated himself as a host on the TV show Tailgating with Kato since 2011 and most recently, appeared in FX comedy series Baskets and a movie B.C. Butcher in 2016.
