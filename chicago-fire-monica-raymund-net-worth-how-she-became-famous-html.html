Monica Raymund Net Worth and How She Became Famous

April 2024 · 3 minute read

Chicago Fire fans know Monica Raymund as Gabby Dawson. Many became invested in her character and followed her dramatic relationship journey with Matt Casey (Jesse Spencer). Some are still hoping Gabby will return to the series and reunite with her ex-husband.

What is Monica Raymund’s net worth? We’ll give you the answer to that question, in addition to a look back at her career and how she became famous.

Monica Raymund on ‘Chicago Fire’

Raymund began playing Gabby Dawson on Chicago Fire in 2012. Her character was first introduced during Chicago Fire Season 1 Episode 1, which aired October 10 of that year. She was a paramedic on Ambulance 61. Dawson and Casey used to be married, but they are no longer together. The former couple divorced after she moved to Puerto Rico. Before getting involved with Casey, Dawson dated a few men on the show, including Jay Halstead and Peter Mills. Dawson and Peter broke up after it became clear she had feelings for Casey.

Dawson is no longer part of the firehouse, but her presence is still felt. She was recently heard from after she called Casey to ask about Mouch’s award. This initially caused problems between Casey and Sylvie Brett (Kara Killmer). Brett was initially hesitant to move forward with Casey because she believed he wasn’t completely over his ex-wife.

When Dawson called Casey at the firehouse, Brett thought this confirmed her worst fears. It turns out, she had nothing to be worried about. Casey later told her during the Chicago Fire Season 9 finale that after speaking to Dawson one more time, he realized what he really wanted. His desire is to be with Brett because he loves her.

Monica Raymund’s net worth

As of this writing, Raymund has an estimated net worth of $4 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Monica Raymund’s movies and TV shows

One of Raymund’s early acting roles was in the 2007 short film Fighter, in which she played the role of Sarah. The following year, she played Trini Martinez in an episode of Law & Order: SVU titled “Authority.” In 2009, Raymund joined the cast of Lie to Me. She played Ria Torres for 48 episodes until the show ended in 2011. Raymund is also known for her appearances in The Good Wife, Chicago P.D., and Hightown.

Is there still hope for Matt Casey and Gabby Dawson on ‘Chicago Fire’?

When asked during an interview if she thought there was still hope for Casey and Dawson, Raymund didn’t seem optimistic. According to her, their romance is pretty much done. She doesn’t believe they will rekindle their romance.

“With Matt Casey, we see the love is still very much alive there, but they kind of know that their paths are finished,” Raymund tells TV Fanatic. “Their journey with each other, it’s finished. There will always be love there. So, who knows if she’ll ever come back?”
