Drake gives lucky fan $30k Birkin Bag but gentleman gesture afterward is praised

April 2024 · 2 minute read

Drake has been making headlines with all the ongoings at his It’s All A Blur North American Tour, where tickets don’t come cheap. However, it seems like those front-row concert tickets paid off for one fan, as the Marvin’s Room singer gifted her a Birkin Bag.

The music star was performing in Los Angeles on tour when the gifting happened. Previous LA nights have drawn in famous guests including Kim Kardashian and Kendall Jenner, who was spotted ‘making out’ with rumored boo Bad Bunny.

Drake gifts fan Birkin bag at LA concert

After being gifted bras by fans on tour, it looks like Drake has returned the favor as he’s only gone and gifted one lucky fan a pink Birkin bag, costing around $30,000, and understandably, the video is now going viral on TikTok.

In the clip, the Shot For Me singer can be seen scouting out the crowd as he chooses one lucky woman to receive the designer bag as he says: “Make sure she has security on the way out.”

Earlier on in the video, he can be heard saying: “Drake ain’t cheap,” as the crowd erupts into cheers. He definitely proved that right.

The rapper makes an effort to interact with his fans during every show and was previously heard seemingly shading Kanye West as he commented on one fan’s Yeezy’s.

Fans can’t get over the lavish gift

Of course, upon seeing the viral video, fans couldn’t help but express their envy over in the comments.

“Can we talk about how he made sure the girl got security out the show to her car!?! the consideration this man has!!” exclaimed one fan.

“All of a sudden, I’m a Drake fan,” joked one user.

One shocked user simply wrote: NOOOOOOO WAYYYYY!”

“I love how he picks a specific girl because if he just threw it away, then girls would be killing each other,” claimed another.

That user may not be wrong, as, at a previous show, Drake could be seen rushing to defend a fan after a fight broke out after the star threw his sweaty towel into the crowd.
