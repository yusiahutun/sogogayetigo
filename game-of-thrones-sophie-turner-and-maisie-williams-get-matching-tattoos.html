Game of Thrones stars Sophie Turner and Maisie Williams got matching tattoos

April 2024 · 2 minute read

    stars Sophie Turner and Maisie Williams are renowned for being inseparable, and now the pair have cemented their friendship by opting for matching tattoos. Hitting the Emmy awards red carpet on Sunday night, Sophie admitted that she and her co-star have gone for matching inkings to celebrate their time together so far on the hit TV show.

    While both of their characters hae survived six seasons of the show so far, is notorious for killing off main characters in shock plot twists – something which Sophie quipped had prompted her to get the tattoo done.

    "We don't know if we're going to make it, so Maisie and I were like, 'let's get these ones before anyone kills us'," she joked.
