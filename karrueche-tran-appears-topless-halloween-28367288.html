Karrueche Tran appears topless for Halloween in stunning robotic warrior queen costume

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Karrueche Tran put on a faux topless display as she partied early for Halloween in Los Angeles on Saturday night.

The 34-year old Hollywood actress stunned as a robotic warrior queen in completely metallic silver - including a breast plate, which tricked the eyes into appearing as an open chest.

Karrueche looked out of this world in the busty display, which incorporated sci-fi style body armour, a mini skirt and knee high boots.

Karrueche, who boasts an impressive 12.7 million Instagram followers, has recently hosted Upcycle Nation - a new fashion competition show.

Discussing the programme, she explained to KTLA: "It’s a fashion competition show about repurposing and upcycling old items. A lot of household items and creating new wearable, beautiful pieces.

"I hope it influences people to understand the effect (fast fashion) has on our planet and to get creative and work with old garments or old household pieces."

Talking about the standard of contestants, she added: "We had so many talented designers on the show, and they created some beautiful pieces I would absolutely wear."

It's not the first time Karrueche has dazzled at this spooky time of year.

Seven years ago to the day she stepped out as Lil Kim in the City of Angels - complete with blue hair to copy the video for her hit single Crush On You.

Wearing the electric blue wig, a giant furry jacket and a matching leotard, the star certainly embodied the rapper's colourful character.

But what really grabbed attention was the fact that Karrueche's backside was on show for all to see.
