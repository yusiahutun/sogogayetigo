Where to Go for Cinco de Mayo in New York City

October 2024 · 2 minute read

1) El Vez: Located in Battery Park City, El Vez combines modern Mexican cuisine in a lively atmosphere, with an outdoor terrace for al fresco dining. Try the crab tostados or zucchini blossom & corn quesadillas with one of the over 150 choices of mezcals and tequilas. For Cinco de Mayo, the all-female mariachi band Flor de Toloache will perform.

2) Lazy Point: For those more set on finding the perfect cocktail for Cinco de Mayo, the recently opened Lazy Point is a beach inspired get away in the heart of downtown New York—driftwood lighting and beachside furniture included. Proenza Schouler hosted their most recent NYFW afterparty at the cocktail bar. The Underhill Tab includes a mix of millagro tequila, lillet rose, lime cointreau and pineapple juice.

3) Empellon Al Pastor: This Saint Mark's Place spot serves a selection of tacos ranging from spit roasted pork with pineapple to oyster mushrooms a la Mexicana. Pair your tacos with an agave root beer or a spicy cucumber margarita, in the restaurant's homey atmosphere.

4) Tijuana Picnic: Tijuana Picnic may just be one of New York's chicest restaurants at the moment. It's a regular hotspot for creatives and people who work in fashion. The food and drinks are excellent: try the duck empanadas, grilled corn with cricket aioli or short rib skewers with one of the many cocktail options.

Media Platforms Design Team

5) Los Tacos No.1: If you're looking for less of a party but still have a craving for quick , fresh and authentic Mexican food on Cinco de Mayo, Los Tacos No. 1 offers the perfect taco haven. Hidden away in Chelsea Market, the décor is charmingly vintage. The menu is simple but includes interesting options like grilled cactus tacos and quesadillas and has no shortage of Mexican sodas.
