Zodiac Signs as FriendsWhich Type of Friend Are You?

July 2024 · 9 minute read

Within the intricate web of human relationships, friends are the thread that bind us together. And when you consider the type of friend your zodiac sign is known for being, you’ll understand the way these threads weave a unique network of personas. “You got your freshmen, ROTC guys, preps, J.V. jocks,” not trying to quote Mean Girls, but the same way the characters of this iconic 2004 comedy embody the colorful array of personalities that make up social cliques, friendships also contribute their one-of-a-kind energy into our lives. From the audacity of your Aries BFF to the bougie happy hours you attend as your Taurus coworker’s plus one, one thing’s for sure: the stars don’t lie.

If you’re familiar with your big three—your sunsign, moon sign, and rising sign—then it doesn’t hurt to reference back to your birth chart when considering the type of friend you are. Your Venus sign is no exception, as it represents everything from your values to your love language. This includes how you approach relationships, as well as the types of connections you attract and enjoy making. On another note, you’ll want to be sure to check for the zodiac sign that rules your 11th house of associations, community affairs, and sense of belonging in the world. If you’re not familiar with the astrological houses, don’t be discouraged—you’ve come to the right place.

The Type of Friend You Are, According to Your Zodiac Sign:

Are you more of a ride-or-die or a mediator? Are you more of a fire-starter or a gossip? Here’s what your zodiac sign says about the type of friendship you offer:

  • Aries: You're the Instigator

    Some may call you a fire starter, but you’re that one friend with the sheer audacity to turn up the heat when everyone else is too hesitant to make the first move. You never cease to lead the way fearlessly and unapologetically, and this is all thanks to your ravishing planetary ruler, Mars. Your fiery spirit ignites passion in every aspect of life, daring and encouraging your besties to embrace the thrill of the moment. This explains why you’re also a master at hyping them up, ensuring they feel confident and ready to take over the world (as your sidekick). On the dark side, your competitive streak can be downright ferocious, leaving your opponents shaking in their boots and your friends simultaneously impressed and slightly terrified.

  • Taurus: You're the Rock

    I know what you’re thinking, but no—you’re not Dwayne Johnson. Ironically enough, he’s also a Taurus, but I digress. In addition to being an incredibly solid and dependable individual, you’re more often than not the steady force within your friend group, the one everyone can count on no matter what. This definitely comes with the turf when you’re ruled by harmony-seeking Venus, but you have this amazing ability to keep calm and carry on when everything else seems to be falling apart. If one of your besties happens to be in a crisis, you’re always there, steady as a rock, with your grounding presence. We also can’t forget about your #nofilter ways. Sure, your stubborn streak is unrelenting, but hey, at least everyone knows where they stand with you.

  • Gemini: You're the Gossip 

    Before you roll your eyes—hear me out. Your ability to perpetually stay in the know is straight up impressive. After all, savvy Mercury is your planetary ruler, so it’s safe to say that having you around is equivalent to a WiFi connection with turbo speed. Did I mention your riveting text messaging skills? From buzzing celebrity news to the latest drama in the workplace, you always bring the tea. And though you have no problem stirring the pot, this isn’t about spreading rumors but about your desire to collect and share information (preferably hard data) with the world. A phenomenal storyteller with a twinkle of mischief, it’s no wonder your besties can’t help but look forward to dinner, drinks, and dishing the dirt.

  • Cancer: You're the Care Package

    You’re often portrayed as the ultimate nurturer among the zodiac signs, but this fiercely protective energy is no different when you’re around your group of friends. You’re ruled by the moon, so everything from your emotional receptivity to your eerily accurate intuition explains why you’re everyone’s go-to friend (not to mention mom-friend) in times of need. Naturally emphatic and deeply sentimental, your mere presence is an eternal source of comfort and support. Whether it’s a shoulder to cry on or a heartfelt word of advice, you always know what to say. Your occasional mood swings, however, are often difficult to comprehend, but even in those moments, your friends never doubt the love in your heart. It’s your hall pass when you refuse to leave the house.

  • Leo: You're the Ringleader

    You strut through life with confidence and flair, leading the pack with a captivating charisma that’s impossible not to love. In addition to your group-selfie-taking skills, your sunny presence and playful persona make every moment with friends feel like a party. Granted, your role as the ringleader goes beyond your grand gestures of love and ongoing excuses to celebrate. It’s your unwavering spirit, and loyalty that knows no bounds. To say you’re popular among friends would be an understatement, but rest assured, you go to great lengths to keep your circle small. This can explain why there’s an undeniable sense of exclusivity that comes with being your friend. Your love is unconditional, and that’s a privilege. 

  • Virgo: You're the GPS

    What would we do without you, Virgo? As if your logistic-loving mind wasn’t enough to make everyone in the room feel safe, your sharp precision and attention to detail explain why you’re the ultimate lifeline. It also helps to know you carry anti-bacterial gel in your purse at all times, and are never not on time. Whether planning an epic celebration or tediously organizing the perfect night out with the girls, your mercurial curations never disappoint. On the contrary, your impeccable sense of direction and voice of reason keep everyone on track. And while there’s no such thing as perfect, your brilliant mind comes pretty close. Your dark side, however, often stems from your critical nature. Just one look is enough.

  • Libra: You're the Mediator

    On top of choosing to avoid confrontation at all costs, you also have an uncanny ability to find common ground and bring harmony to any situation. Being friends with you is like having a walking, talking, aesthetically pleasing diplomat in our midst. Your knack for seeing things from multiple perspectives explains why you’re the ultimate mediator friend. Did I mention you’re a total delight to be around? Whether it’s settling a disagreement or the way you bring sweetness to a tense situation, you’re always there to guide others toward resolution. On the dark side, your ongoing quest for balance could lead you to neglect your own needs, which could eventually lead to resentment. So, no need to bend over backward to please others.

  • Scorpio: You're the Enigma

    The mysterious friend that keeps everyone on their toes, there’s never a dull moment when you’re around, Scorpio. Your enigmatic aura and mystifying presence add a thrilling edge to every interaction, leaving the friend group intrigued and a tad bit intimidated for that matter. Unraveling the multiple layers of your personality is similar to solving a puzzle, intricate and deeply complex. As for your psychic abilities? This only adds an extra layer to your Plutonian mystique, which contributes to your desire for soul-to-soul connection as opposed to superficial friendships, considering you have no problem seeing what lies beyond the veil. On the dark side, the intensity of your demeanor can be intimidating to some, leaving them feeling uncertain about where they stand with you.

  • Sagittarius: You're the YOLO

    It’s no secret that “you only live once” is your life motto, especially with Jupiter (The Greater Benefic) as your planetary ruler. This can also explain why your often seen as the happy-go-lucky friend who’s always up for adventures and daring escapades. Your free-spirited nature and boundless enthusiasm for new experiences also make you the ultimate travel companion, bringing excitement to every moment. In your eyes, it’s never too late for a moonlight drive, and your friends know that all they have to do is say the word, and you’re ready for an impromptu road trip. The downside of being the YOLO friend lies in the potential for recklessness and impulsivity. While your daring spirit is admirable, it can also result in risky behavior and disregard for consequences.

  • Capricorn: You're the Boss

    You mean business, Capricorn. Your unwavering ambition and desire for authority prove why you’re the CEO and boss of your friend group. With your strategic thinking and laser-focused determination, you always have a game plan. Your besties know that when they need someone to take charge and lead the way, you’re the one to turn to. However, being the boss isn’t just about calling the shots and making tough decisions; it’s also about inspiring your peers to reach their fullest potential. Your biggest pet peeve? Friends who don’t hold themselves accountable. You value responsibility and integrity above all else, and you expect the same from those around you. Unfortunately, this relentless drive for achievement can sometimes overshadow your ability to relax and enjoy life’s simple pleasures.

  • Aquarius: You're the Contrarian

    You’re a rebel without a cause, Aquarius. On top of the way, you shamelessly march to the beat of your own drum, and you enjoy flipping the script when your friends least expect it. Similar to your enlightening planetary ruler, Uranus, your unconventional thinking and sci-fi-loving ways make you the sparkling intellectual of the group, challenging outdated beliefs and societal norms. While everyone sticks to the traditional path, you’re off exploring new galaxies and ideas. This is why many often can’t help but fall for your unique blend of wit and eccentricity. Granted, being the contrarian of the group can rub others the wrong way, but your unique perspective continues to keep things interesting.

  • Pisces: You're the Artist

    Just like your elusive and all-encompassing planetary ruler, Neptune, you embody artistic expression with your boundless imagination. As the artist of the friend group, you possess a unique ability to channel your emotions and experiences into beautiful works of art that touch the hearts and souls of those around you. You see the world through rose-colored glasses and somehow find a way to project this magic outward into your external reality. Be it with your incredible music taste or beautiful photographs, you aim to make your fantasies and dreams a reality for yourself, and those around you. This could also come with challenges, as you may struggle to balance your ideals with the harsh realities of life. Remember to lean on your peers for support.
