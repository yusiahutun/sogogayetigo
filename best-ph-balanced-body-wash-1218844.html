    Skin Type

    Knowing your skin type is crucial when it comes to choosing the right body wash. If you have oily skin, look for a ph neutral body wash that is oil-free and won't clog your pores. Whereas, if you have dry skin, the best ph balanced body wash for you is the one that is moisturizing and won't strip your skin of its natural oils.     FAQ's

    What is the best pH balanced?

    The ideal pH balance for your skin is between 4.5 and 5.5. This is because the skin's natural pH balance is slightly acidic. When the pH balance of your skincare products matches the skin's natural pH, it helps to maintain a healthy skin barrier and prevents moisture loss.

    Which pH is best for your skin?

    As mentioned earlier, the best pH for your skin is around 5.5. This pH level is slightly acidic and is the most optimal for healthy skin. It helps to maintain the skin's natural protective barrier and keeps it hydrated. When your skin's pH is balanced, it can better protect itself from external factors like pollution and bacteria.

    What pH will damage skin?

    The pH that will damage the skin can vary from person to person, but generally, a pH below 4 or above 9 can be harmful to the skin.
