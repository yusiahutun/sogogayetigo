Early Life and Education

Michael Imperioli was born on March 26, 1966, in Mount Vernon, New York. He grew up in an Italian-American family and developed a passion for acting at a young age. Imperioli attended Sacred Heart High School and later studied acting at the Stella Adler Conservatory in New York City.
