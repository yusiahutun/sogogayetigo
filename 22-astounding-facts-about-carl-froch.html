Carl Froch won a total of four world titles during his career. He held the WBC super middleweight title, the WBA (Super) super middleweight title, the IBF super middleweight title, and the WBA (Regular) super middleweight title.

3. What is Carl Froch’s boxing record?

Carl Froch had an impressive professional boxing record of 33 wins, with 24 of those victories coming by knockout. He suffered only 2 losses in his career.

4. Did Carl Froch ever fight in the United States?

Yes, Carl Froch had notable fights in the United States. One of his most memorable bouts took place in the United States against Andre Ward in the Super Six World Boxing Classic final.

Yes, Carl Froch officially announced his retirement from professional boxing in 2015. Since retiring, he has stayed connected with the sport through his work as a commentator and analyst.
