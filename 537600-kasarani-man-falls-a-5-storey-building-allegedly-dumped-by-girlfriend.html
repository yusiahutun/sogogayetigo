Kasarani: Man Falls Off a 5-Storey Building after Allegedly Being Dumped by Girlfriend

April 2024 · 2 minute read
  • A middle-aged man fell from the fifth floor of an apartment in Kasarani under unclear circumstances
  • In the video, the man is spotted on the top balcony of an apartment moments before falling to the ground
  • Concerned Kenyans who recorded the incident said they could not save him as he had locked the door

Amos Khaemba, a journalist at TUKO.co.ke, brings over three years of experience covering politics and current affairs in Kenya.

Nairobi - Police in Kasarani have launched investigations after a man fell off an apartment in the Kasarani area under mysterious circumstances.

How Kasarani residents recorded the incident

On Saturday, February 10, the man, whose identity remains unknown, jumped off a five-storey building after he allegedly fell out with his girlfriend.

In a viral video seen by TUKO.co.ke, the man is said to have jumped off an apartment in the Kasarani area after locking the house from inside.

In the video, the middle-aged man is spotted on the top balcony of an apartment, where he seems to be having a few thoughts before he finally decides to jump out of the apartment.

Concerned Kenyans near the scene recorded the incident, while others could be heard saying they could not access the man's room to save him as the door was locked.

Mombasa man dies after falling from hotel

In other sad news, a 23-year-old man tragically lost his life after leaping from a four-storey hotel located in the Bondeni Old Town area, situated within the Mombasa Central Business District.

According to Mombasa County Police Commander Stephen Matu, he sustained internal injuries in the stomach and in the head.

“He suffered internal injuries on his head and stomach and succumbed to his injuries at around 1030 hrs,” Matu told NTV.
