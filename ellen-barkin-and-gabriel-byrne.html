About

Gabriel Byrne is a 73 year old Irish Actor. Born Gabriel James Byrne on 12th May, 1950 in DuWalkinstown, Republic of Ireland, he is famous for The Usual Suspects. His zodiac sign is Taurus.

Ellen Barkin is a 69 year old American Actress. Born Ellen Rona Barkin on 16th April, 1954 in The Bronx, New York City, New York, USA and educated at High School of Performing Arts, she is famous for her roles as Beth Schreiber in the 1982 comedy-drama film Diner, Anne Osborne in the 1986 neo-noir romantic thriller film The Big Easy, Helen Cruger in the 1989 neo-noir thriller film Sea of Love, Amanda Brooks in the 1991 fantasy comedy film Switch, Glory Marie Jackson in the 1997 drama television film Before Women Had Wings, Abigail Sponder in the 2007 heist comedy film Ocean's Thirteen, Nana in the NBC comedy series The New Normal, and Janine “Smurf” Cody in the critically-acclaimed TNT crime drama series Animal Kingdom. Her zodiac sign is Aries.
