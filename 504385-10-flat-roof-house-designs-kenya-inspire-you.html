Once a preserve of the Coastal people due to Arabic cultural influence, flat roof house designs seem to be gaining popularity in the Kenyan mainland. Most developers and homeowners are now turning to this house design because it has the allure of modernity and minimalism. So, what are the best flat roof house designs in Kenya?

Flat roof house designs are often built horizontally with a bit of incline, meaning they are not entirely flat. They have a minimal slope that helps with water drainage. These house designs are primarily used in arid areas where the roof space can be used for relaxation and shelter. They are one of the most notable roofing systems in Egyptian, Arabian, and Persian architecture.
